## Card Enrollment

1. In the **Config Tool**, click the **Cardholder management** under **Tasks**.

   <Image src='/img/integration/genetec/card-enrollment.png' alone />

1. Click the **New** button from the bottom.

   <Image src='/img/integration/genetec/new-ico.png' alone />

1. Enter the **First name** and **Last name**, then click the **Add a credential** button. In the popup that appears, click **Automatic entry**.

   <Image src='/img/integration/genetec/card-enrollment-add-credential1.png' alone />

1. Select the **Door** assigned to the Suprema device as a reader, then tag a card to the device.

   <Image src='/img/integration/genetec/card-enrollment-add-credential2.png' alone />

1. The card is automatically enrolled to the credential.

   <Image src='/img/integration/genetec/card-enrollment-add-credential3.png' alone />

## Face Enrollment

### Precautions when Enrolling Face

When enrolling your face for authentication, refer to the picture below to enroll your face correctly.

If you do not follow the on-screen instructions, face enrollment may take a long time or authentication may fail.

* When enrolling a face, maintain a distance of 60-100 cm between the device and the face.

* Make sure that your expression does not change.

* Do not wear a mask, hat, sunglasses, or eye patches.

* Do not raise head up or lower head.

* Do not wear thick makeup.

* Do not close your eyes.

* Make sure that both of your shoulders correctly appear on the screen.

* Do not use a corrected picture.

* Stand still and enroll your face by staring at the screen.

* Be careful not to display two faces on the screen. Make sure to enroll one face at a time.

* Do not enroll your face in dark places.

<Image src='/img/integration/genetec/face-enrollment.png' width='80%' alone />

:::info

* **Resolution**: Larger than 250 x 250 pixels

* **Image size**: Up to 10 MB

* **Supported format**: JPG, JPEG or PNG only

:::

### Enroll from Device

1. In the cardholder details window, click **Suprema Enrollment**.

   <Image src='/img/integration/genetec/enroll-from-device1.png' alone />

1. Click the **Face-Scan**.

   <Image src='/img/integration/genetec/enroll-from-device2.png' alone />

1. Select the Suprema face authentication device to enroll the face credential.

   <Image src='/img/integration/genetec/enroll-from-device3.png' alone />

1. Click the **+Add** button to create a face template, then click the **Scan** button and scan the face on the device.

   <Image src='/img/integration/genetec/enroll-from-device4.png' alone />

1. Check the face has been properly scanned, and then click the **Enroll** button to enroll the face.

### Enroll Face Template with Genetec Profile Photo

1. In the cardholder details window, click **Suprema Enrollment**.

   <Image src='/img/integration/genetec/enroll-from-device1.png' alone />

1. Click the **Face-Scan**.

   <Image src='/img/integration/genetec/enroll-from-device2.png' alone />

1. Select the Suprema face authentication device to enroll the face credential.

   <Image src='/img/integration/genetec/enroll-from-device3.png' alone />

1. Click the **+Add** button to create a face template, then check **Use Genetec Photo** to automatically upload the user profile photo. The Suprema device extracts face templates from user profile photos.

   <Image src='/img/integration/genetec/enroll-from-device5.png' alone />

1. Check the uploaded photo, and then click the **Enroll** button to enroll the face.

## Bulk Enrollment

1. Go to **Suprema Device Plugin** → **Global Settings**.

   <Image src='/img/integration/genetec/global-settings1.png' alone />

1. Click the **Bulk Face Upload** button. You can check the Face Extraction progress.

   <Image src='/img/integration/genetec/global-settings2.png' alone />

   :::info
   
   Face template extraction is performed only for GSC cardholders who have a profile image.

   :::

1. Once the process is completed, you can check the results.

   * Show Failed Users: View the list of users for whom face extraction failed.

   * Face Template Extraction Report: View the report related to face template extraction.

## How to Search for a Cardholder with a Face Template

1. Click the **Advanced search** on the **Cardholder management** page.

   <Image src='/img/integration/genetec/advanced-search.png' alone />

1. Click the **Custom fields**, switch it to **On**, and click **Add criterion**. Then, select the **BiometricsTimestamp**.

   <Image src='/img/integration/genetec/advanced-search-custom-fields1.png' alone />

1. Select the **Specific range** and set the period to search.

   :::note
   
   **Example**

   * **From**: Plugin installation date

   * **To**: Today

   :::

   To search by specific time, click **Set time** and enter the time.

   <Image src='/img/integration/genetec/advanced-search-custom-fields2.png' alone />

1. Click the **Search** button.

1. All cardholders who have face templates appear.
