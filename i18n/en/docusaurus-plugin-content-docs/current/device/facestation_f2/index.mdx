FaceStation F2 is a multimodal access control terminal equipped with next-generation fusion facial authentication technology, optimized for the demands of contactless security solutions in the post-COVID era. Suprema's unique fusion matching technology utilizes visual recognition and infrared (IR) recognition simultaneously to achieve industry-leading authentication accuracy, boasting an exceptional false acceptance rate (FAR) of one in ten billion.
