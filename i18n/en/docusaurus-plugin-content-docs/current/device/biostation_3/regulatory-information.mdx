Provides information on regulations and certifications complied with by the product.

## Regulatory & Licenses

You can check the license, regulatory certification, and compliance marks on the product.

* CE / FCC / IC / KC / UKCA / RCM / MIC / TELEC

1. Press <Image src='/img/common/ico-menu.svg' ico alone /> and authenticate with the Admin level credential.

2. Touch <Cmd sid='ui_109' product='dev' /> in the upper right corner and touch <Image src='/img/common/ico-regulatory.svg' ico alone /> to check <Cmd sid='ui_077' product='dev' />.

   <Image src='/img/device/bs3-regulatory-licenses.png' />

:::info

If an administrator is not designated, pressing <Image src='/img/common/ico-menu.svg' ico alone /> will display the <Cmd sid='ui_109' product='dev' /> screen.

:::
