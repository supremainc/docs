# Firmware Version 1.6.2 (Build No. 1.6.2_201202)

Release<Separator/>2020-12-02

## Bug Fixes

---

<BugLists>

1. After the firmware of both a master device and a slave device was upgraded to the version 1.6.x., the reconnection between devices failed when rebooting the slave device. (BEW2-OAP, BEW2-OHP, BEW2-ODP)

1. Delay occurred intermittently when reading a FeliCa card. (BEW2-OAPB, BEW2-OHPB, BEW2-ODPB)

</BugLists>
