BioEntry W2 is an outdoor fingerprint access control device based on Suprema's latest fingerprint authentication technology and hardware platform. Equipped with a class-leading 1.2GHz quad-core CPU, it offers ultra-fast authentication performance and quick startup speed, employing Suprema's next-generation fingerprint recognition algorithm and Live Finger Detection technology to provide enhanced security performance. Additionally, it supports both 125kHz and 13.56MHz frequency RFID cards simultaneously, allowing it to read various cards such as EM, HID Prox, MIFARE, iCLASS, DESFire, and FeliCa, and its IP67 rated waterproof and dustproof design along with IK09 rated impact resistance makes it easy to install in outdoor environments.
