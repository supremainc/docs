# Firmware Version 1.1.0 (Build No. 1.1.0_180317)

Release<Separator/>2018-03-26

## New Features and Improvements

---

1. Support Interlock zone.

1. Support Reset without Network Settings.

1. Support Daylight Saving Time setting.

1. Improves Trigger & Action for duress finger.

1. Improves to handle the encryption key of the important information stored in database differently from server to server.

1. Support the secure tamper.

1. Support ISO14443A 10 Byte CSN.

1. Support connecting with BioLite N2, XPass D2.

## Bug Fix

---

<BugLists>

1. Issue where RS-485 disconnect when changing home screen of the slave device to user's logo.

1. Problem that the slave device is disconnected when the slave device finishes booting more slowly than the master device with the RS-485 encryption key set.

1. Issue if the elevator is configured as a fire alarm zone, relay operation will be turned off when the alarm is released after the fire alarm.

1. Fixed to retain previous rate when setting to the unsupported RS-485 rate.

1. Problem that the duration of the supervised input behaves differently from the setting in Trigger & Action.

1. Fixed to output the logs that the input information and the door open information of the intrusion sensor in the intrusion alarm zone.

1. Problem that delay time of Action Signal Output is not applied in the slave device.

</BugLists>
