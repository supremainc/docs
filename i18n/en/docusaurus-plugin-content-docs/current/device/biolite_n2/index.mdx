BioLite N2 is an outdoor fingerprint recognition access control device based on Suprema's latest fingerprint recognition technology and hardware platform. Equipped with a class-leading 1.2GHz CPU, it offers ultra-fast authentication performance and quick startup speed, and applies Suprema's next-generation fingerprint recognition algorithm to provide enhanced security performance. Additionally, it supports 125kHz and 13.56MHz frequency RFID simultaneously, allowing it to read various cards such as EM, HID Prox, MIFARE, iCLASS, DESFire, FeliCa, NFC, and 2.4GHz BLE, and the IP67 rated waterproof and dustproof design makes it easy to install outdoors.
