XPass Q2 is built for fast, high-traffic visitor and ticketing access. Its smart, QR/barcode–first design and multi-tech credential support keep people moving seamlessly, while its compact, durable form makes deployments simple across busy entrances. XPass Q2 delivers a low-effort, high-reliability access experience for facilities that need speed, flexibility, and minimal upkeep.
