The X-Station 2 is an intelligent integrated terminal for access control and attendance management, designed for broad RFID card compatibility and fast, accurate Fingerprint authentication. It uses a built-in camera to read QR codes and barcodes, and optionally supports mobile access cards. Secure Boot, OSDP over RS-485 communication, and image logs enhance security and operational reliability, while Power over Ethernet (PoE), a 4-inch capacitive touchscreen, and an IP65-rated design simplify installation.
