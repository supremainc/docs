This guide explains how to reset the camera to factory settings and replace the desiccant module.

## Factory default {#factory-reset}

Deletes all information stored on the camera and resets all settings.

1. After connecting the camera to power, locate the reset button.

   <Image src='/img/camera/factory-reset-vionyx.svg' className='none' alone />

2. Press and hold the reset button for at least 5 seconds, then release it.

3. After the factory reset completes, the camera will restart automatically.

:::caution

Performing a factory reset will delete all user data and settings, including network settings.

:::

:::info

The IP address remains unchanged even after a factory reset.

:::

## Replacing the desiccant module

A desiccant module is installed inside the camera to prevent lens condensation. To maintain dehumidification performance, we recommend replacing the desiccant module at least every 1 year.

1. Check the mounting location of the desiccant module.

2. Loosen the screw by turning it counterclockwise, then remove the desiccant module from the cover.

   <Image src='/img/camera/change-silicagel-vionyx.png' className='none' width={600} alone />

3. Place the new desiccant module on the cover, then turn the screw clockwise to secure it.

:::caution

Replacement intervals may vary depending on the installation environment.

:::

:::info

* Record the replacement date to keep track of the next replacement.

* Replacement desiccant modules can be purchased from the retailer where you bought the product.

:::
