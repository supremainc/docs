## Fixing the bracket and the product

### General bracket

1. Determine the correct position to install the bracket using the provided drilling template.

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-recommended-bs3.svg' className='none' alone />

   <InstallationBracketNotes height={136} />

2. Secure the bracket tightly using the fixing screws at the location where the product will be mounted.

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-device1-bs3.svg' className='none' width={280} alone />

   :::info

   * If installing the product on a concrete wall, drill a hole, insert a PVC anchor, and secure it with a fixing screw.

   * To avoid RF interference, a minimum separation distance must be maintained.

   <Columns>
   <Column className='text--center'>

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-wall1-bs3.png' className='none' width='60%' />

   <div className='overflow-x'>

   | Wall thickness | Distance |
   | :-: | :-: |
   | 100 mm | 200 mm |
   | 120 mm | 180 mm |
   | 150 mm | 150 mm |

   </div>

   </Column>

   <Column className='text--center'>

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-wall2-bs3.svg' className='none' width='300' />

   </Column>
   </Columns>

   * When using a mobile access card, install devices maintaining a minimum distance of 1 m between devices to avoid BLE interference.

   :::

3. After attaching the cable cover, tighten the six screws on the cover to secure it firmly.

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-device2-bs3.svg' className='none' width={350} alone />

   :::info

   Make sure that the cable cover is completely closed after connecting it to the product to maintain the water-resistant and dust-resistant features (IP65 rating).

   :::

4. Mount the product on the fixed bracket.

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-device3-bs3.svg' className='none' width={400} alone />

5. Rotate the fixing screws to assemble the product with the bracket.

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-device4-bs3.svg' className='none' width={120} alone />

   :::info

   When assembling the product with the bracket, you can use the included bracket fixing screw (Star Shaped) instead of the product fixing screw for enhanced security.

   :::

### Tilting Bracket

Use the dedicated tilting bracket to adjust the authentication angle to suit the installation environment. The tilting bracket is sold separately.

1. Determine the correct position to install the bracket using the provided drilling template.

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-recommended-bs3.svg' className='none' alone />

   <InstallationBracketNotes height={136} />

2. Use the tilting bracket mounting screws to secure the back of the tilting bracket firmly to the installation location (wall).

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-device-tilt_bracket1-bs3.svg' className='none' width={300} alone />

   :::note

   Depending on the installation location, install the tilting bracket with the top and bottom orientation as follows.

   * When installing on the left side of the entrance: Install so the "R TOP" marking faces upward

   * When installing on the right side of the entrance: Install so the "L TOP" marking faces upward

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-device-tilt_bracket0-bs3.svg' className='none' width={700} alone />

   :::

   :::info

   * If installing the product on a concrete wall, drill a hole, insert a PVC anchor, and secure it with a fixing screw.

   * To avoid RF interference, a minimum separation distance must be maintained.

   <Columns>
   <Column className='text--center'>

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-wall1-bs3.png' className='none' width='60%' />

   <div className='overflow-x'>

   | Wall thickness | Distance |
   | :-: | :-: |
   | 100 mm | 200 mm |
   | 120 mm | 180 mm |
   | 150 mm | 150 mm |

   </div>

   </Column>

   <Column className='text--center'>

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-wall2-bs3.svg' className='none' width='300' />

   </Column>
   </Columns>

   * When using a mobile access card, install devices maintaining a minimum distance of 1 m between devices to avoid BLE interference.

   :::

3. Join the front of the tilting bracket to the groove in the back, close it, and secure it with two screws in the upper and lower screw holes.

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-device-tilt_bracket2-bs3.svg' className='none' width={550} alone />

   :::info

   If you tighten the screws too much, the angle cannot be adjusted. Tighten the screws to the proper level.

   :::

4. After attaching the cable cover, tighten the six screws on the cover to secure it firmly.

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-device2-bs3.svg' className='none' width={350} alone />

   :::info

   Make sure that the cable cover is completely closed after connecting it to the product to maintain the water-resistant and dust-resistant features (IP65 rating).

   :::

5. Secure the wall-mount bracket firmly on top of the tilting bracket.

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-device-tilt_bracket3-bs3.svg' className='none' width={400} alone />

6. Mount the product on the secured wall-mount bracket.

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-device-tilt_bracket4-bs3.svg' className='none' width={400} alone />

7. Tighten the product fixing screws to assemble the product and the wall-mount bracket.

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-device-tilt_bracket5-bs3.svg' className='none' width={200} alone />

   :::info

   When assembling the product with the bracket, you can use the included bracket fixing screw (Star Shaped) instead of the product fixing screw for enhanced security.

   :::

8. Adjust the tilting bracket to the desired angle.

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-device-tilt_bracket6-bs3.svg' className='none' width={300} alone />
