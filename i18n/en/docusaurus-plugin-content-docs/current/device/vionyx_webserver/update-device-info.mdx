Check basic camera information and enter the device name, description, and location to manage multiple cameras in your organization.

Update camera information in the following situations:

* Rename an installed camera to a distinctive name within your organization.

* Add additional information about the camera's location or installation site.

* Record the camera's precise location by entering GPS coordinates.

* Add specific notes or descriptions for each camera when managing multiple cameras.

:::info

* Fields such as model name, serial number, and hardware version are read-only and cannot be edited.

* Camera name, memo, and location support Unicode and accept Korean, English, numbers, and special characters.

:::

## Check basic camera information

Check basic information such as the camera model, serial number, and hardware version.

1. Click the <Cmd sid='system' product='cam' /> menu in the upper-left corner of the screen.

2. Click <Cmd sid='device_info' product='cam' /> in the left sidebar.

   <ProdImg src='/img/cam-server/cam-webserver-system-device-info.png' />

You can check the basic information below in the <Cmd sid='information' product='cam' /> section. These fields are read-only and cannot be edited.

* <Cmd sid='model' product='cam' />: Camera model

* <Cmd sid='device_sn' product='cam' />: Camera serial number

* <Cmd sid='hw_version' product='cam' />: Hardware version

* <Cmd sid='kernel' product='cam' />: Kernel version

* <Cmd sid='open_source_license' product='cam' />: Click <Cmd sid='view' product='cam' /> to view open source license information.

## Set camera information

Enter the camera name, memo, and location to manage cameras effectively.

1. Click the <Cmd sid='system' product='cam' /> menu in the upper-left corner of the screen.

2. Click <Cmd sid='device_info' product='cam' /> in the left sidebar.

### Enter name

Enter a custom name to make the camera easy to identify. Enter the camera name in the <Cmd sid='name' product='cam' /> input field.

* Up to 64 characters.

* Allows Korean, English, numbers, and special characters (`.`, `-`, `(`, `)`).

> Example: "Entrance 1", "Conference Room A"

:::info

The entered camera name appears in the system menu and throughout the web UI, making it easy to identify the camera.

:::

### Add memo

Enter additional information or a memo for the camera. Enter a memo or description in the <Cmd sid='memo' product='cam' /> input field. Up to 64 characters.

> Example: installation department, administrator name, installation date

### Enter location information

Enter the camera's installation location as text. Enter the camera's installation location in the <Cmd sid='location' product='cam' /> input field. Up to 64 characters.

> Example: "1st floor lobby entrance", "2nd floor conference room waiting area"

### Save settings

After entering all information, click <Cmd sid='save' product='cam' /> in the upper right corner of the screen.

## Set GPS coordinates

Enter the camera's exact geographic location as GPS coordinates.

1. Click the <Cmd sid='system' product='cam' /> menu in the upper-left corner of the screen.

2. Click <Cmd sid='device_info' product='cam' /> in the left sidebar.

3. To enable GPS setting, click the <Cmd sid='gps' product='cam' /> toggle in the <Cmd sid='information' product='cam' /> section.

   <ProdImg src='/img/cam-server/cam-webserver-system-device-info-gps.png' />

4. Enter the longitude and latitude information.

   > Example: Seoul (37.5665, 126.9780)

5. Click <Cmd sid='save' product='cam' /> in the upper right corner of the screen to save the setting.

:::info

* Enter GPS coordinates in decimal degrees (DD) format.

* To disable GPS setting, click the <Cmd sid='gps' product='cam' /> toggle again.

:::
