Upload audio clips for the camera and apply them as alarm tones or voice prompts. Upload and manage audio files in WAV format.

Upload audio clips in the following situations.

* When you need to upload alarm tones to play when an event occurs

* When you need to add multilingual voice prompts

* When you need to add voice messages for specific situations

## Check audio storage capacity

1. Click the <Cmd sid='system' product='cam' /> menu in the upper-left corner of the screen.

2. Click <Cmd sid='audio' product='cam' /> in the left sidebar.

3. You can check the remaining capacity of the audio storage in the <Cmd sid='audio_clip' product='cam' /> section.

<ProdImg src='/img/cam-server/cam-webserver-system-audio-check-size.png' />

## Prepare audio files

Before uploading audio clips, check the following.

* **File format**: Only the `.wav` extension is supported.

* **Sample rate**: Use a sample rate of 48 kHz or higher.

* **File name**: Use only English letters, numbers, and underscores (`_`), up to 64 characters. Spaces and special characters are not allowed.

* **File size**: You can upload files within the remaining capacity.

## Upload audio clips

1. Click the <Cmd sid='system' product='cam' /> menu in the upper-left corner of the screen.

2. Click <Cmd sid='audio' product='cam' /> in the left sidebar.

3. Click <Cmd sid='add_button' product='cam' /> at the top right of the screen.

   <ProdImg src='/img/cam-server/cam-webserver-system-audio-add-btn.png' />

4. The <Cmd sid='add_clip_file' product='cam' /> popup appears.

   <ProdImg src='/img/cam-server/cam-webserver-system-audio-add-popup.png' />

5. Drag and drop a WAV file into the upload area, or click <Cmd sid='choose_file' product='cam' /> and select a WAV file stored locally.

6. After the upload completes, adjust the volume and repeat settings options.

   <ProdImg src='/img/cam-server/cam-webserver-system-audio-add-set-option.png' />

7. Click <Cmd sid='firmware_upload' product='cam' /> to upload the audio clip.

View uploaded audio clips in the list.

<ProdImg src='/img/cam-server/cam-webserver-system-audio-complete.png' />

:::info

* Adjust the volume slider to set the playback volume for the audio clip.

* To play the audio clip, click <IcPlay title='Play' />.

:::

## Delete audio clip

Click <IcCamTrash title='Delete' /> to the right of the audio clip you want to delete.

<ProdImg src='/img/cam-server/cam-webserver-system-audio-delete.png' />

:::info

* If the audio clip you want to delete is applied to an AI rule, it is automatically excluded from the AI rule settings.

* For more information on AI rule settings, see <DocLink docId='device/vionyx_webserver/set-ai-rule' />.

:::
