Configure AI events to monitor specific situations detected by the camera. Combine AI events—person tracking, intrusion detection, line crossing, and people counting—to meet security and operational requirements. AI rules automatically execute actions when events occur, such as audio playback or sending webhooks.

:::info

To use AI events, set at least one facial area that applies to all events.

:::
