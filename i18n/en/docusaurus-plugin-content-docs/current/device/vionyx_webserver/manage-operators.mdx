In addition to administrators, add, edit, or delete operator accounts with limited permissions. Operators can manage certain camera features on behalf of administrators.

An operator account is required to use the ONVIF feature. When connecting to the camera from an ONVIF client or VMS, use a registered operator account.

Add operators in the following situations.

* Assign additional staff for camera operation.

* Grant only specific permissions.

* When multiple people must collaborate to manage the camera.

* Grant team members read-only or edit permissions.

:::info

* Add up to 10 operators.

* Operators cannot add other operators. Only administrators can add operators.

* Operator passwords follow the same rules as administrator passwords. For more information, refer to <DocLink docId='device/vionyx_webserver/update-admin-password' />.

* Each operator can change only their own account information.

:::

## Operator permission types

Select one of the following two permissions when adding an operator.

* <Cmd sid='read_only' product='cam' className='bold' />: View all camera settings and logs; cannot change settings.

* <Cmd sid='read_write' product='cam' className='bold' />: Access all menus except the Administrator menu; can change camera settings. Operators can change their own password but not other operators' or administrators' passwords.

## Add operator

1. Click the <Cmd sid='system' product='cam' /> menu in the upper-left corner of the screen.

2. Click <Cmd sid='account_management' product='cam' /> in the left sidebar.

   <ProdImg src='/img/cam-server/cam-webserver-system-account-manage.png' />

3. In the <Cmd sid='operator' product='cam' /> section, click <Cmd sid='add_button' product='cam' />.

4. When the <Cmd sid='add_operator' product='cam' /> popup appears, enter the required information.

   <ProdImg src='/img/cam-server/cam-webserver-system-add-operator.png' />

   * <Cmd sid='id' product='cam' />: Enter the operator's unique ID.

     * Up to 64 characters.

     * Can include Korean, English letters, and numbers.

     * Allowed special characters: `_`, `.`, `-`, `(`, `)`.

     * Must be unique and not duplicate another operator.

   * <Cmd sid='password' product='cam' />: Enter the operator's password. Follows the same rules as the administrator password. For more information, refer to <DocLink docId='device/vionyx_webserver/update-admin-password' />.

   * <Cmd sid='confirm_password' product='cam' />: Re-enter the password.

   * <Cmd sid='permission' product='cam' />: Select the operator's permission.

   * <Cmd sid='enabled' product='cam' />: Enable or disable the operator's account.

5. After entering all information, click <Cmd sid='ok' product='cam' />.

After adding a new operator, the new account appears in the list.

:::info

* After adding an operator, log in with that account to verify permissions are applied correctly.

* Deliver the operator's ID and password to the operator securely.

:::

## Change operator permissions

Change an operator's permissions.

1. Click the <Cmd sid='system' product='cam' /> menu in the upper-left corner of the screen.

2. Click <Cmd sid='account_management' product='cam' /> in the left sidebar.

3. In the <Cmd sid='operator' product='cam' /> section, click the <Cmd sid='permission' product='cam' /> field for the operator whose permissions you want to change.

   <ProdImg src='/img/cam-server/cam-webserver-system-manage-operator.png' />

4. Select the desired permissions from the list.

When the permission change succeeds, a success message appears in the upper right corner of the screen.

:::tip

Click <IcCamEdit title='Edit' /> to open the <Cmd sid='edit_operator' product='cam' /> window. In the <Cmd sid='permission' product='cam' /> field, change to the desired permissions, and then click <Cmd sid='ok' product='cam' />.

<ProdImg src='/img/cam-server/cam-webserver-system-manage-operator-edit.png' width='50%' />

:::

## Disable operator

Temporarily disable an operator's account. Blocks access without deleting the account.

1. Click the <Cmd sid='system' product='cam' /> menu in the upper-left corner of the screen.

2. Click <Cmd sid='account_management' product='cam' /> in the left sidebar.

3. In the <Cmd sid='operator' product='cam' /> section, click the toggle in the <Cmd sid='enabled' product='cam' /> field for the operator you want to deactivate.

   <ProdImg src='/img/cam-server/cam-webserver-system-manage-operator-active.png' />

When deactivation succeeds, a success message appears in the upper right corner of the screen. Disabled operator accounts cannot log in.

:::tip

Click <IcCamEdit title='Edit' /> to open the <Cmd sid='edit_operator' product='cam' /> window. Click the toggle in the <Cmd sid='enabled' product='cam' /> field to deactivate the account. Click <Cmd sid='ok' product='cam' /> to save the changes.

<ProdImg src='/img/cam-server/cam-webserver-system-manage-operator-edit.png' width='50%' />

:::

:::info

Disabled accounts can be reactivated later.

:::

:::caution

If an operator is already logged in, they will be denied access on their next login attempt.

:::

## Delete operator

You can permanently delete operator accounts. Deleted accounts cannot be recovered.

1. Click the <Cmd sid='system' product='cam' /> menu in the upper-left corner of the screen.

2. Click <Cmd sid='account_management' product='cam' /> in the left sidebar.

3. In the <Cmd sid='operator' product='cam' /> section, click <IcCamTrash title='Delete' /> for the operator you want to delete.

   <ProdImg src='/img/cam-server/cam-webserver-system-manage-operator-delete.png' />

4. When the warning message appears, click <Cmd sid='ok' product='cam' />.

When deletion succeeds, a success message appears in the upper right corner of the screen. The operator account is removed from the list.
