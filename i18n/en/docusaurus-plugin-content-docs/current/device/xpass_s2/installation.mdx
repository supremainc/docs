Provides the complete installation procedures and connection examples required for the device.

## Fixing the bracket and the product

### General bracket

1. Secure the bracket tightly using the fixing screws at the location where the product will be mounted.

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-device1-xps2.png' className='none' alone />

   :::info

   If installing the product on a concrete wall, drill a hole, insert a PVC anchor, and secure it with a fixing screw.

   :::

2. Mount the product on the fixed bracket.

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-device2-xps2.png' className='none' alone />

3. Rotate the fixing screws to assemble the product with the bracket.

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-device3-xps2.png' className='none' alone />

   :::info

   When assembling the product with the bracket, you can use the included bracket fixing screw (Star Shaped) instead of the product fixing screw for enhanced security.

   :::

### Extension Bracket

1. Assemble the standard bracket and extension bracket using the fixed screws included with the extension bracket.

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-device-bracket1-xps2.png' className='none' alone />

2. Secure the extension bracket firmly in position using the fixed screws.

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-device-bracket2-xps2.png' className='none' alone />

   :::info

   If installing the product on a concrete wall, drill a hole, insert a PVC anchor, and secure it with a fixing screw.

   :::

3. Mount the product on the secured extension bracket.

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-device-bracket3-xps2.png' className='none' alone />

4. Screw the product screws to assemble the product and extension bracket.

   <Image src='/img/device/installation-device-bracket4-xps2.png' className='none' alone />

   :::info

   When assembling the extension bracket and product, you can use the included bracket fixing screws (star-shaped) instead of the product screws for enhanced security.

   :::
