X-Station 2 is a versatile multi-technology terminal for access control and workforce management, built around broad RFID card compatibility and fast, accurate fingerprint authentication. For compatibility, it also reads QR codes and barcodes via the built-in camera and supports Mobile Access when needed. Security and operations are reinforced by secure boot, Open Supervised Device Protocol (OSDP) over RS-485, and image logs, while Power over Ethernet (PoE), a 4-inch capacitive touchscreen, and an IP65-rated design simplify installation.
