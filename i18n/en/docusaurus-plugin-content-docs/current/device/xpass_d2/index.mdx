XPass D2 is an outdoor compact RFID reader optimized for centralized access control systems with Suprema's CoreStation. It supports a variety of RFID cards and mobile cards using Suprema's multi-RFID technology and patented latest mobile card technology utilizing NFC and BLE. It features an IP67 waterproof and dustproof design as well as an IK08 impact-resistant design, providing robust durability, and offers various options depending on the installation environment, such as door frames or switch boxes.
