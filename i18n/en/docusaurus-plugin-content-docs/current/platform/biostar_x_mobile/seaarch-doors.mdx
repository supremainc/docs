When the door list is long and it is hard to find the door you need, use search and filtering to find it quickly.

## Search doors

<Columns>
<Column className='width--23'>

<Image src='/img/mobile/bsx-mobile-app-door-search.png' alone />

</Column>
<Column className='padding--l40'>

Search by door name, group name, or assigned device name.

1. Tap <Cmd>DOOR</Cmd> on the app home screen.

   :::note

   You can also access it through the side menu. Tap <IcHamberg title='Hamburger icon' width={20} height={20} /> → <Cmd>Door</Cmd>.

   :::

2. Tap <IcSearch title='Search' width='20px' height='20px' /> at the top of the door list.

3. Enter a search term in the search field. Search by door name, group name, or assigned device name.

4. After you finish entering the search term, tap <IcSearch title='Search' width='20px' height='20px' />.

Doors that match the search term appear in the search results. Tap the door you want in the search results to open door details.

:::info

* Recent search history appears below the search input field. Tap a recent search entry to search again with that term.

* To close the search input field, tap <IcClose title='Close' width='20px' height='20px' />.

:::

</Column>
</Columns>

## Filter door list

<Columns>
<Column className='width--23'>

<Image src='/img/mobile/bsx-mobile-app-door-filter.png' alone />

</Column>
<Column className='padding--l40'>

Filter the door list by door group.

1. Tap <Cmd>DOOR</Cmd> on the app home screen.

   :::note

   You can also access it through the side menu. Tap <IcHamberg title='Hamburger icon' width={20} height={20} /> → <Cmd>Door</Cmd>.

   :::

2. Tap <IcAppFilter title='Filter' width='20px' height='20px' /> at the top of the door list.

3. Tap <Cmd>All Door Groups</Cmd>.

4. Select the desired group from the door group list.

   <Image src='/img/mobile/bsx-mobile-app-door-filter-select-group.png' width='252px' alone />

5. After you finish selecting a door group, tap <Cmd>Yes</Cmd>.

6. To filter the door list with the selected criteria, tap <Cmd>Apply</Cmd>.

Doors in the selected group appear in the list.

:::info

To reset the filter conditions, tap <IcAppLogFiltered title='Filtered' width='20px' height='20px' /> → <IcAppReset title='Reset' width='20px' height='20px' /> in the upper right corner of the screen.

:::

</Column>
</Columns>
