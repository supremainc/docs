You can change the password for an administrator account in the BioStar X mobile app. Change the administrator account password regularly to enhance account security.

<Columns>
<Column className='width--23'>

<Image src='/img/mobile/bsx-mobile-app-account-change-pw.png' alone />

</Column>
<Column className='padding--l40'>

1. On the app home screen, tap <IcHamberg title='Hamburger icon' width={20} height={20} /> in the upper-left corner.

2. Tap <Cmd>Change Password</Cmd> in the side menu.

3. Enter the current password in the <Cmd>Current Password</Cmd> field.

4. Enter the new password in the <Cmd>New Password</Cmd> and <Cmd>Confirm password</Cmd> fields, in that order.

5. Tap <Cmd>Change Password</Cmd> to complete the password change.

</Column>
