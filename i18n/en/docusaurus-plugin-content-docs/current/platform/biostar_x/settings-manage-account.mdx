This guide describes how to assign operator permissions and add and configure custom permissions to enrolled users. Operator permissions control the tasks a user can perform on **Biostar X**. **Custom permissions** allow the setting of additional permissions beyond the default operator permissions.

## Check permission types

Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page. Click <Cmd sid='setting.menu.account.root' /> on the left sidebar. Refer to the provided accounts based on permission levels.

## Granting operator permissions to users {#addUserPermission}

Guide on granting operator permissions to general users.

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click <Cmd sid='setting.menu.account.root' /> on the left sidebar.

3. Click the desired account type from the account list.

4. Click <Cmd sid='button.plusAdd' product='2' /> in the <Cmd sid='settings.account.addUser' product='2' /> option.

5. Select the user to whom you want to grant operator permissions from the user list.

6. After selecting all desired users, click <Cmd sid='button.apply' product='2' /> at the bottom of the screen.

:::info

* If itâ€™s difficult to find users due to their quantity, click <IcSearch /> to search and select the desired user.

* Operator permissions can also be granted when adding or modifying user information. Please refer to [the following](set-permission) for details.

* If permissions have already been set when adding or modifying user information, they will be included in the <Cmd sid='settings.account.addUser' product='2' /> option.

:::

## Excluding operator permissions

Guide on excluding operator permissions granted to users.

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click <Cmd sid='setting.menu.account.root' /> on the left sidebar.

3. Click the desired account type from the account list.

4. Click <IcTrash2 /> for the user from whom you wish to exclude permissions in the <Cmd sid='settings.account.addUser' product='2' /> option.

5. Click <Cmd sid='button.apply' product='2' /> at the bottom of the screen to save the settings.

## Checking operator permission details

Click the account in the account list in the <Cmd sid='setting.menu.account.root' /> menu to view the details of the operational permissions for that account.

* <Cmd sid='common.name' product='2' />: The name of the selected operator permission.

* <Cmd sid='settings.accountList.columnDesc' product='2' />: A brief description of the permission.

* <Cmd sid='settings.account.menu.detailSetting' product='2' />: Can view the groups granted permissions item by item.

* <Cmd sid='settings.account.menu.setting' product='2' />: Can view the permissions granted to access the menu.

* <Cmd sid='settings.account.addUser' product='2' />: Can view users granted permissions.

  * <Cmd sid='common.editRead' product='2' />: Grants permission to add, edit, or delete items in the menu.

  * <Cmd sid='common.read' product='2' />: Grants permission to enter the menu and only view the settings.

:::info

For methods to grant permissions to specific users, refer to [the following](#addUserPermission).

:::
