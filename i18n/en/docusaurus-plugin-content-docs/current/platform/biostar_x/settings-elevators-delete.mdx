This guide describes how to delete registered elevators.

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click <Cmd sid='setting.menu.elevator.root' /> in the left sidebar.

3. Click the checkbox of the elevator you want to delete from the elevator list.

4. Click <Cmd sid='button.delete' product='2' /> at the top right of the screen.

The selected elevator will be deleted. Deleted elevators cannot be recovered.

:::info

<Cmd sid='button.delete' product='2' /> is activated only when one or more elevators are selected from the elevator list.

:::
