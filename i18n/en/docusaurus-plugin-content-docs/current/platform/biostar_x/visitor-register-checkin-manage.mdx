Check the visitors in check-in status and modify visit information or process check-out.

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.visitor' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click the <Cmd sid='visitor.status.checkIn' product='2' /> tab at the top of the screen.

   <Image src='/img/biostarx-visitor-register-checkin-list.png' />

3. Click the visitor in the list to modify or check out.

4. Check the visitor's visit request information.

   <Image src='/img/biostarx-visitor-register-checkin-edit.png' />

   * To modify and save individual items, click the <Cmd sid='common.edit' product='2' /> button at the bottom right of the screen.

   * To process check-out, click the <Cmd sid='button.checkout' product='2' /> button at the bottom right of the screen.

:::info

You can also select visitors to check out from the <Cmd sid='visitor.status.checkIn' product='2' /> list. Click the checkbox for the visitor to check out in the list and then click the <Cmd sid='button.checkout' product='2' /> button at the top right of the screen.

<Image src='/img/biostarx-visitor-register-checkin-list-checkout.png' />

:::
