This guide describes how to delete enrolled doors.

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click <Cmd>Door</Cmd> on the left sidebar of the screen.

3. Click the checkbox of the door you want to delete from the list.

4. Click <Cmd sid='button.delete' product='2' /> at the top right of the screen.

The selected door will be deleted. Deleted doors cannot be recovered.

:::info

<Cmd sid='button.delete' product='2' /> is activated only when one or more doors are selected from the list.

:::
