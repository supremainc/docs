Configure floors before setting up zones and facilities. Upload the floor layout as an image and place doors and cameras for visual management. After configuring a floor, check the doors and cameras for that floor on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.arena' /> page.

<Image src='/img/biostarx-settings-map-add-floor-example.png' alone caption />

:::info

* Register doors before configuring floors. For more information on enrolling doors, refer to the [following](settings-door-add).

* For more information on map monitoring, refer to the [following](monitoring-map).

:::

## Add floor {#add-floor}

Upload a floor layout or a 3D bird’s eye view image to add a floor and place doors and cameras.

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> and then click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' />.

2. In the left sidebar, click <Cmd sid='setting.menu.map.root' /> → <Cmd sid='setting.menu.map.floor' />.

3. Click <Cmd>+ New Floor</Cmd> in the top right corner of the floor list screen.

4. Enter the name and description of the floor you want to add in the <Cmd sid='setting.floor.page.title' /> screen's <Cmd sid='setting.floor.information.section.title' /> section.

5. Click <Cmd>Floor Plan</Cmd> in the <Cmd sid='setting.floor.configuration.section.title' /> section or drag and drop the image you want to upload.

6. In the <Cmd>Add Floor Plan</Cmd> window, review the uploaded image and complete the settings.

    <Image src='/img/biostarx-settings-map-add-floor-01.png' alone />

    * To rotate the image, click <IcLrot /> or <IcRrot />.

    * To crop the image, click <IcCrop />.

7. After finishing the image settings, click <Cmd>Apply</Cmd>.

8. To add doors to the uploaded image, right-click at the desired location on the image and select <Cmd>Add Door</Cmd>.

    <Image src='/img/biostarx-settings-map-add-floor-02.png' alone />

9. In the <Cmd>Add Door</Cmd> window, select one of the enrolled doors.

    <Image src='/img/biostarx-settings-map-add-floor-03.png' alone />

10. Click <Cmd>Apply</Cmd> to place the door on the image.

    <Image src='/img/biostarx-settings-map-add-floor-04.png' alone />

11. To add a camera to the uploaded image, right-click at the desired location on the image and select <Cmd>Add Camera</Cmd>.

    <Image src='/img/biostarx-settings-map-add-floor-05.png' alone />

12. In the <Cmd>Add Camera</Cmd> window, select one of the enrolled cameras.

13. Click <Cmd>Apply</Cmd> to place the camera on the image.

    <Image src='/img/biostarx-settings-map-add-floor-06.png' alone />

14. Additionally place doors and cameras as needed in the actual floor.

15. After completing all settings and saving the configured floor, click <Cmd sid='common.apply' />.

## Modify floor configuration

You can modify the layout of the floor with added doors and cameras. Modifying the floor layout allows you to change or delete the positions of doors and cameras.

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> and then click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' />.

2. In the left sidebar, click <Cmd sid='setting.menu.map.root' /> → <Cmd sid='setting.menu.map.floor' />.

3. Click the floor you want to modify from the list.

After completing all settings and saving the configured floor, click <Cmd sid='common.apply' />.

### Delete door/camera

Click the door or camera you want to delete from the floor image. The <IcFremove /> icon will appear. Click the icon to delete the selected door or camera.

### Set camera shooting range

Right-click the camera on the floor image. The shooting range will be displayed.

<Image src='/img/biostarx-settings-map-floor-camera-setting-01.png' alone />

* To change the shooting range, click and drag in the desired direction while holding the <IcCamrot /> icon.

  <Image src='/img/biostarx-settings-floor-camera-rotation.gif' alone />

* To adjust the shooting range, click and drag the corner edges of the range to the desired size.

  <Image src='/img/biostarx-settings-floor-camera-adjust.gif' alone />

* To delete the shooting range, click the <IcFremove /> icon.

:::caution

The settings made by the user are provided purely for the convenience of security management and do not reflect the actual shooting range of the cameras.

:::

### Delete floor image

To delete a floor image, right-click the floor image and select <Cmd>Delete Floor Plan</Cmd>.

## Delete floor {#delete-floor}

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> and then click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' />.

2. In the left sidebar, click <Cmd sid='setting.menu.map.root' /> → <Cmd sid='setting.menu.map.floor' />.

3. Click the checkbox of the floor you want to delete from the list. You can select more than one floor.

4. Click <Cmd sid='common.delete' /> in the top right corner of the screen.

The selected floors will be deleted from the list. The deleted floors cannot be recovered.
