This guide describes how to set up groups for easy management of multiple doors. A door group is a feature that allows you to manage several doors as one group. By setting up groups, you can configure doors in bulk.

:::tip

Register group names using door locations or office names for easier management.

:::

## Add door group {#adding-door-group}

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click <Cmd>Door</Cmd> on the left sidebar of the screen.

3. Right-click on <Cmd sid='menu.door.allDoorGroups' product='2' /> from the door list.

    <Image src='/img/biostarx-setting-door-add-group.png' />

4. Click <Cmd sid='door.addDoorGroup' product='2' /> in the popup menu.

    <Image src='/img/biostarx-setting-door-add-group-naming.png' />

5. Enter the desired group name.

:::info

* You can create up to 8 levels of door groups.

* The door group name can be up to 48 characters long.

:::

## Rename door group {#renaming-door-group}

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click <Cmd>Door</Cmd> on the left sidebar of the screen.

3. Right-click on the group you want to rename from the door list.

    <Image src='/img/biostarx-setting-door-rename-group.png' />

4. Click <Cmd sid='door.renameDoorGroup' product='2' /> in the popup menu.

5. Enter the new group name.

:::info

The door group name can be up to 48 characters long.

:::

## Delete door group {#deleting-door-group}

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click <Cmd>Door</Cmd> on the left sidebar of the screen.

3. Right-click on the group you want to delete from the door list.

    <Image src='/img/biostarx-setting-door-rename-group.png' />

4. Click <Cmd sid='door.deleteDoorGroup' product='2' /> in the popup menu.

:::caution

Deleting a door group removes all doors included in the group. To avoid deleting the doors, move them to another group before deleting the group.

:::

## Add door to group {#adding-door-to-group}

### Add from door list

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click <Cmd>Door</Cmd> on the left sidebar of the screen.

3. Select the door you want to add to a group from the door list and drag it to the desired group.

    <Image src='/img/biostarx-setting-door-to-group.png' />

The selected door moves to the group.

<Image src='/img/biostarx-setting-door-to-group-result.png' />

### Add from door information

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click <Cmd>Door</Cmd> on the left sidebar of the screen.

3. Click the door you want to add to a group from the list on the right side of the screen.

    <Image src='/img/biostarx-setting-door-to-group-list.png' />

4. When the door information edit screen appears, click the <Cmd sid='common.group' product='2' /> option in the <Cmd sid='common.information' product='2' /> section.

    <Image src='/img/biostarx-setting-door-to-group-info-select.png' />

5. Select the desired group.

6. Click <Cmd sid='button.apply' product='2' /> at the bottom of the screen.
