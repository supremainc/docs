Access levels enable users to set times for entry and grant permission to access doors during those times. Access levels are a key element for configuring access groups.

## Before start {#beforeStart}

Initialize doors before enrolling access levels. Refer to the [following](settings-door-add) for more details on door enrollment.

## Enroll access level {#addAccessLevel}

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click <Cmd>Access Control</Cmd> in the left sidebar.

3. Click <Cmd sid='button.func.addAccessLevel' product='2' />.

4. Enter <Cmd sid='common.name' product='2' /> and <Cmd sid='common.description' product='2' /> in the <Cmd sid='accessGroup.addNewAccessLevel' product='2' /> screen.

    <Image src='/img/biostarx-settings-add-access-level.png' />

5. Select the door to apply the access level in the <Cmd sid='common.door' product='2' /> field.

6. Select the schedule to apply the access level in the <Cmd sid='common.schedule' product='2' /> field.

7. Click <Cmd sid='button.apply' product='2' /> at the bottom of the screen to enroll the access level.

:::info

* Up to 128 access levels can be enrolled per access group.

* Click <IcSearch /> in the <Cmd sid='common.door' product='2' /> and <Cmd sid='common.schedule' product='2' /> fields to search for the desired doors and schedules.

* Click <IcTrash2 /> to delete items added in the <Cmd sid='common.door' product='2' /> and <Cmd sid='common.schedule' product='2' /> fields.

* If the desired door is not available, you need to enroll a new door. Refer to the [following](settings-door-add) for more details on door enrollment.

* If the desired schedule is not available, click <Cmd sid='button.plusAddSchedule' product='2' /> to add one. Refer to the [following](settings-schedule) for more details on schedule configuration.

:::

## Modify access level {#editAccessLevel}

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click <Cmd>Access Control</Cmd> in the left sidebar.

3. Click the <Cmd sid='access_control.tab.access_level' product='2' /> tab.

4. Click the access level you wish to modify from the access level list.

5. Modify the desired items.

6. Click <Cmd sid='button.apply' product='2' /> at the bottom of the screen to complete the modification.

## Delete access level {#deleteAccessLevel}

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click <Cmd>Access Control</Cmd> in the left sidebar.

3. Click the <Cmd sid='access_control.tab.access_level' product='2' /> tab.

4. Check the checkbox of the access level you wish to delete from the access level list.

5. Click <Cmd sid='button.deleteAccessLevel' product='2' /> at the top right of the screen.
