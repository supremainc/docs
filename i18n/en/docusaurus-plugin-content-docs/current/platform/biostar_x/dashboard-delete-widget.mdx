Delete widgets added to the dashboard.

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.dashboard' /> in the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click the <IcSet2 /> icon in the upper right corner of the screen.

3. When you enter <Cmd sid='reportApp.dashboardSettings' product='2' /> mode, click the <IcTrash2 /> button in the upper right corner of the **Widget** you want to delete.

4. Check the delete confirmation popup and click the <Cmd sid='reportApp.yes' product='2' /> button.

   <Image src='/img/biostarx-dashboard-widget-del.png' className='none' />

5. Click <Cmd sid='reportApp.apply' product='2' /> in the upper right corner of the <Cmd sid='reportApp.dashboardSettings' product='2' /> screen.

Confirm that the changes made on the dashboard are reflected.
