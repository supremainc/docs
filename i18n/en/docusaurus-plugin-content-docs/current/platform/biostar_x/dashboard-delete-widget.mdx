Delete widgets added to the dashboard.

1. On the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page, click the <Cmd>Dashboard</Cmd>.

1. Click <IcSet2 /> in the upper right to enter <Cmd sid='reportApp.dashboardSettings' product='2' />.

1. Click <IcTrash2 /> at the top right of the **Widget** you want to delete.

    <Image src='/img/biostarx-dashboard-widget-del.png' className='none' caption />

1. Check the deletion confirmation pop-up and click <Cmd sid='reportApp.yes' product='2' />.

1. Click <Cmd sid='reportApp.apply' product='2' /> at the top right of the <Cmd sid='reportApp.dashboardSettings' product='2' /> screen to apply the deletion.
