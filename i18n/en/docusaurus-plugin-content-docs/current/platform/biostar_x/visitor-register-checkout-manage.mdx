Check the visitors in checkout status and use their visit application information to register a repeat visit.

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.visitor' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click the <Cmd sid='visitor.status.checkOut' product='2' /> tab at the top of the screen.

   <Image src='/img/biostarx-visitor-register-checkout-list.png' />

3. Click the visitor you want to register again from the visitor list.

4. Click the <Cmd sid='common.edit' product='2' /> button at the bottom right of the screen.

5. If there are items that need correction, modify the individual items and then click the <Cmd sid='button.enroll' product='2' /> button.

<Image src='/img/biostarx-visitor-register-checkout-edit.png' />

The re-registered visitor will be added to the visit application list. Click the <Cmd sid='visitor.status.register' product='2' /> tab at the top of the screen to check.
