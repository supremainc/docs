Roll Call quickly generates a personnel list based on entry records within a specific time and place. When an emergency occurs, the system can determine whether a user has arrived at the roll call location by authenticating through the entry or exit device at a predesignated location.

Can be used for emergency response, safety checks, and providing rescue team information. This allows for efficient management of personnel status utilizing entry and exit records.

:::info

Roll call settings can be used through additional options available with an **Advanced** license or above. For more information on licensing policies, see <DocLink docId='platform/biostar_x/licensing#features-add-ons' />.

:::

## Register roll call {#addRollcallZone}

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click <Cmd sid='setting.menu.advancedAc.root' /> in the left sidebar.

3. Click <Cmd sid='button.func.addZone' product='2' />.

4. Select <Cmd sid='zone.rollCall' product='2' /> and click <Cmd sid='button.apply' product='2' />.

5. Enter the name and description of the roll call setting to be added in the <Cmd sid='common.info' product='2' /> section.

   <Image src='/img/biostarx-settings-adv-ac-add-rollcall.png' />

6. Set details in the <Cmd sid='device.info.configuration' product='2' /> section. For more information, see <DocLink inner='#setting' />.

7. After completing all settings, click <Cmd sid='button.apply' product='2' /> at the bottom of the screen.

:::info

<Cmd sid='common.name' product='2' /> can accept up to 48 characters and cannot be set with the same name as other settings.

:::

## Setting options guide {#setting}

The details for roll call settings are as follows.

<Image src='/img/biostarx-settings-adv-ac-add-rollcall-config.png' />

* <Cmd sid='zone.globalZone' product='2' />: You can check the scope of application. Roll call settings are supported only in <Cmd sid='zone.globalZone.global' product='2' /> mode, and can be configured for all devices registered in **BioStar X**.

* <Cmd sid='zone.pause.activatedAndDeactivated' product='2' />: You can activate or temporarily deactivate the settings.

* <Cmd sid='zone.rollCall.manualStatusChange' product='2' />: You can set it to change the status manually.

* <Cmd sid='zone.entryDevices' product='2' />: Select the device to be used when entering. You can choose from the list of registered devices.

* <Cmd sid='zone.exitDevices' product='2' />: Select the device to be used when exiting. You can choose from the list of registered devices.

* <Cmd sid='common.users' product='2' />: You can select user groups or individual users who are allowed to enter.

:::info

* The same device cannot be set for both <Cmd sid='zone.entryDevices' product='2' /> and <Cmd sid='zone.exitDevices' product='2' /> at the same time.

* You can select up to 10 devices for <Cmd sid='zone.entryDevices' product='2' /> and <Cmd sid='zone.exitDevices' product='2' />.

* For more information about device registration, refer to the following.

  * <DocLink docId='platform/biostar_x/settings-adding-devices' />

  * <DocLink docId='platform/biostar_x/settings-adding-wiegand' />

  * <DocLink docId='platform/biostar_x/settings-adding-slave-device' />

:::

## Activate/deactivate {#activateDeactivateOccupancyZone}

You can activate disabled roll call settings or deactivate activated settings.

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click <Cmd sid='setting.menu.advancedAc.root' /> in the left sidebar.

3. Click <Cmd sid='zone.rollCall' product='2' /> from the list.

4. Click the checkbox for the item you want to activate or deactivate in the roll call settings list.

5. Click <Cmd sid='zone.pause.activate' product='2' /> or <Cmd sid='zone.pause.deactivate' product='2' /> in the upper right corner of the screen.

## Delete {#deleteOccupancyZone}

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click <Cmd sid='setting.menu.advancedAc.root' /> in the left sidebar.

3. Click <Cmd sid='zone.rollCall' product='2' /> from the list.

4. Click the checkbox for the item you want to delete from the roll call settings list.

5. Click <Cmd sid='button.deleteZone' product='2' /> at the top right of the screen.

6. When the warning message appears, click the <Cmd sid='button.continue' product='2' /> button.

## View roll call reports {#viewRollCallReport}

To check the roll call report, follow these steps.

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> on the page of <Cmd sid='shell.header.data' />.

2. On the left sidebar of the screen, click <Cmd sid='data.sidebar.events' /> → <Cmd sid='data.sidebar.rollCall' />.

3. When the <Cmd sid='data.dialog.rollCall.selectRollCall' /> window appears, select the roll call item you want to check in the report, then click the <Cmd sid='data.button.next' /> button.

   <Image src='/img/biostarx-settings-adv-ac-rollcoll-report-01.png' />

   :::note

   If you do not see the desired roll call item, adjust the period at the top of the screen.

   :::

4. In the option settings window, select the data to include in the report, then click the <Cmd sid='data.button.generate' /> button.

   <Image src='/img/biostarx-settings-adv-ac-rollcoll-report-02.png' />

Generating the report. The report provides information on each user, including their entry time, exit time, last location, and notes.
