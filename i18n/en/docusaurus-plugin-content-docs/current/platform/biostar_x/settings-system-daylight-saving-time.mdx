<Cmd sid='common.dst' product='2' /> (DST) is a function that adjusts the time to better utilize natural daylight.

1. On the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page, click the <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' />.

1. In the left sidebar, click the <Cmd sid='common.dst' product='2' />.

1. Click <Cmd sid="button.plusAdd" product='2' />.

1. After configuring each field, click <Cmd sid="button.add" product='2' />.

    <Image src='/img/biostarx-settings-system-daylight-saving-time.png' className='none' />

1. Click <Cmd sid="button.apply" product='2' /> to save the settings.

:::info

You cannot edit or delete a daylight saving time that is already in use.

:::
