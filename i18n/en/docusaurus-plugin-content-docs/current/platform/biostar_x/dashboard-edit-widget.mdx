Edit widgets added to the dashboard.

1. On the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page, click the <Cmd>Dashboard</Cmd>.

2. Click <IcSet2 /> at the top right to enter <Cmd sid='reportApp.dashboardSettings' product='2' />.

3. Click <IcEdit /> at the top right of the **Widget** you want to edit, then edit the desired part.

   <Image src='/img/biostarx-widget-edit.png' className='none' caption />

4. After making edits, click <Cmd sid='reportApp.apply' product='2' /> to save the changes.

5. After making adjustments to the widgets, select <Cmd sid='reportApp.apply' product='2' />, located at the top-right of the <Cmd sid='reportApp.dashboardSettings' product='2' /> screen, to apply and save the changes.

:::info

* The <Cmd sid='reportApp.widgetTypes' product='2' /> cannot be changed in the <Cmd sid='reportApp.editWidget' product='2' />.

* <Cmd sid='reportApp.widgetTypes.text' product='2' />, <Cmd sid='reportApp.widgetTypes.systemUsage' product='2' />, and <Cmd sid='reportApp.widgetTypes.doorControl' product='2' /> that use a specific name cannot modify the <Cmd sid='reportApp.widgetName' product='2' />.

:::
