Edit widgets added to the dashboard.

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.dashboard' /> in the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click the <IcSet2 /> icon in the upper right corner of the screen. 

3. Click <IcEdit /> at the top right of the **Widget** you want to modify in <Cmd sid='reportApp.dashboardSettings' product='2' /> mode.

4. When the <Cmd sid='reportApp.editWidget' product='2' /> window appears, modify your desired items.

   <Image src='/img/biostarx-widget-edit.png' className='none' caption />

5. Click <Cmd sid='reportApp.apply' product='2' /> after completing your modifications.

6. Click <Cmd sid='reportApp.apply' product='2' /> in the upper right of the <Cmd sid='reportApp.dashboardSettings' product='2' /> screen.

Confirm that the changes made on the dashboard are reflected.

:::info

* The <Cmd sid='reportApp.widgetTypes' product='2' /> cannot be changed in the <Cmd sid='reportApp.editWidget' product='2' />.

* <Cmd sid='reportApp.widgetTypes.text' product='2' />, <Cmd sid='reportApp.widgetTypes.systemUsage' product='2' />, and <Cmd sid='reportApp.widgetTypes.doorControl' product='2' /> that use a specific name cannot modify the <Cmd sid='reportApp.widgetName' product='2' />.

:::
