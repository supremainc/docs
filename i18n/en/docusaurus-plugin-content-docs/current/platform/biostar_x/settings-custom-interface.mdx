Adding a quick action button in the <Cmd sid="common.customInterface" product='2' /> menu allows you to easily control multiple access points at once by clicking the quick action button on the **BioStar X** main screen.

:::info

To add a <Cmd sid="common.triggerAndAction" product='2' /> for Quick Action, refer to [the following](settings-triger-and-action).

:::

## Add quick action button

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. In the left sidebar, click the <Cmd sid="common.customInterface" product='2' />.

   <Image src='/img/biostarx-settings-custom-interface.png' />

3. Click <Cmd sid="common.quickActionLayout" product='2' /> button in the section.

4. When the <Cmd sid='settings.customInterface.quickAction.add' product='2' /> window appears, enter and set each item.

   <Image src='/img/settings-custom-interface-add-quick-action.png' className='none' />

   * <Cmd sid="settings.customInterface.quickAction.operatorLevel" product='2' />: Select the <Cmd sid="settings.customInterface.quickAction.operatorLevel" product='2' /> that can execute the quick action you want to add. You can select multiple options.

   * <Cmd sid="settings.customInterface.quickAction.triggerAction" product='2' />: Select the <Cmd sid="settings.customInterface.quickAction.triggerAction" product='2' /> to execute. You can select multiple options.

   * <Cmd sid="settings.customInterface.quickAction.confirmBeforeRun" product='2' />: Enabling this option will prompt a confirmation popup when you press the <Cmd sid="common.quickAction" product='2' /> button to ask if you want to execute it.

5. Click <Cmd sid="button.apply" product='2' /> button to save the settings.

6. Click Apply at the bottom right of the screen. 

The <Cmd sid="common.quickAction" product='2' /> button added in the upper right header area of the **BioStar X** screen will be created. Click the created <Cmd sid="common.quickAction" product='2' /> button to verify it works as configured in <Cmd sid="common.triggerAndAction" product='2' />.

<Image src='/img/settings-custom-interface-main-quick-action.png' className='none' />

:::info

* You can add up to four <Cmd sid="common.quickAction" product='2' /> buttons in the upper header area of the screen. It is fixed for use on any page.

* The <Cmd sid="common.quickAction" product='2' /> buttons available may differ depending on the settings configured for the user <Cmd sid="user.bioStarOperator" product='2' />.

:::

## Editing the Quick Action buttons

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. In the left sidebar, click the <Cmd sid="common.customInterface" product='2' />.

3. Click the <IcEditUL /> button on the right side of the quick action button you want to modify.

   <Image src='/img/settings-custom-interface-quick-action-layout.png' />

4. When the popup for editing appears, modify the desired item and click the <Cmd sid="button.apply" product='2' /> button.

5. To save the modifications, click the <Cmd sid="button.apply" product='2' /> button at the bottom right of the screen.

## Deleting the Quick Action button

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. In the left sidebar, click the <Cmd sid="common.customInterface" product='2' />.

3. Click the <IcTrash2 /> button to the right of the quick action button you want to delete.

   <Image src='/img/settings-custom-interface-quick-action-layout.png' />

4. Check the deletion confirmation pop-up and click <Cmd sid="button.yes" product='2' />.

   <Image src='/img/settings-custom-interface-confirm.png' className='none' />

5. To reflect the deleted items, click the <Cmd sid="button.apply" product='2' /> button at the bottom right of the screen.
