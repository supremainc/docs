This guide describes how to modify the settings information of the registered access door. Change detailed settings for individual doors or select multiple doors to modify common items in bulk.

## Modify information for a door {#edit-one-door-info}

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click <Cmd sid='setting.menu.door.root' /> in the left sidebar.

3. Click the door you wish to modify in the door list.

4. Modify the details in each section.

5. Once all configurations are complete, click the <Cmd sid='button.apply' product='2' /> button at the bottom of the screen.

:::info

For detailed information on each section of the door modification screen, refer to [the following](settings-door-add).

:::

## Batch edit multiple doors {#edit-multiple-door-info}

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

2. Click <Cmd sid='setting.menu.door.root' /> in the left sidebar.

3. Click the checkboxes for the doors you wish to modify in the door list. Select two or more doors.

4. Click <Cmd sid='button.batchEdit' product='2' /> at the top right of the screen.

5. When the <Cmd sid='button.batchEdit' product='2' /> window appears, set your desired options.

   <Image src='/img/biostarx-setting-door-edit-batch.png' />

   * <Cmd sid='door.openTime' product='2' />: Set the duration for which the door remains open after authentication is complete. The door will automatically lock after this time.

6. After completing all settings, click <Cmd sid='button.apply' product='2' />.

:::info

<Cmd sid='button.batchEdit' product='2' /> is activated only when two or more doors are selected from the door list.

:::
