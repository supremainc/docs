This guide explains how to adding a Wiegand device to a registered master or slave device.

:::info

* <Glossary termid='master_device' />

* <Glossary termid='slave_device' />

* <Glossary termid='wiegand' />

:::

1. Click <Cmd sid='shell.header.setting' /> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page.

1. Click <Cmd>Device</Cmd> in the left sidebar.

1. Right-click on the master or slave device in the <Cmd sid='menu.device.allDeviceGroups' product='2' /> list.

   <Image src='/img/biostarx-setting-device-adding-wiegand.png' />

1. Click <Cmd sid='device.menu.addWiegandDevice' product='2' /> in the popup menu.

1. When the list of Wiegand devices connected to the master device appears, select the device to enroll.

   <Image src='/img/biostarx-setting-device-adding-wiegand-list.png' />

1. Click the <Cmd sid='button.add' product='2' /> button.

1. When the device enrollment confirmation message appears, click the <Cmd sid='button.apply' product='2' /> button.

The enrolled Wiegand device will appear under the selected device.

<Image src='/img/biostarx-setting-device-adding-wiegand-ok.png' />
