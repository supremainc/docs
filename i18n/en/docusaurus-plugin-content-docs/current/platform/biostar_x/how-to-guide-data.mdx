In the <Cmd>Data</Cmd> menu, create and save user information or various events in BioStar X in the desired format.

Automatically generate the desired reports periodically, and the generated reports can be exported or printed as CSV or PDF.

Click <Cmd>Data</Cmd> on the <Cmd sid='shell.header.launcher' /> page or select <Cmd>Data</Cmd> from the shortcut list at the top left of the screen.
