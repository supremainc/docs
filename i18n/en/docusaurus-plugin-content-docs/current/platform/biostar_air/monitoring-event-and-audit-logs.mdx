## <Cmd sid="event_logs_submenu" product="air" className="bold" />

Go to <Cmd sid="event_logs_submenu" product="air" /> to view event logs. Click <Cmd sid="monitoring_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="event_logs_submenu" product="air" /> in the left sidebar. The <Cmd sid="event_logs_submenu" product="air" /> screen shows a table with details including event time and name, door name, device alias, user type, and user name.

<Image src="/img/air/bsair-monitoring-eventlog-list.png" />

## <Cmd sid="audit_logs_submenu" product="air" className="bold" />

Go to <Cmd sid="audit_logs_submenu" product="air" /> to view audit logs. Click <Cmd sid="monitoring_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="audit_logs_submenu" product="air" /> in the left sidebar. The <Cmd sid="audit_logs_submenu" product="air" /> screen displays logs that track administrator actions. The audit log table includes information such as operation time, email address, admin privilege, operation type, menu path, and operation details.

<Image src="/img/air/bsair-monitoring-auditlog-list.png" />

## Sort and manage logs

### <Cmd sid="column_filter_modal_title" product="air" className="normal" />

Customize table columns to show or hide information. Reorder columns to place frequently used ones at the front. Click the <IcAirColumn /> button at the top of the event log list. The <Cmd sid="column_filter_modal_title" product="air" /> window appears.

<Image src="/img/air/bsair-monitoring-eventlog-list-custom-table.png" />

* Select or clear the checkboxes for columns to show or hide.

* To reorder columns, click and drag a column to the desired position.

* Click <Cmd sid="column_filter_modal_save_btn" product="air" /> to save the <Cmd sid="column_filter_modal_title" product="air" /> settings.

* Click <Cmd sid="column_filter_modal_apply_btn" product="air" /> to apply <Cmd sid="column_filter_modal_title" product="air" /> to the log list without saving.

* Click <Cmd sid="column_filter_modal_reset_btn" product="air" /> to cancel the changes in <Cmd sid="column_filter_modal_title" product="air" /> and restore the initial state.

:::info

* When you click <Cmd sid="column_filter_modal_save_btn" product="air" />, the settings remain even after navigating to another menu. When you click <Cmd sid="column_filter_modal_apply_btn" product="air" />, the changes apply only to the current list and revert after you leave the page.

* <Cmd sid="audit_logs_submenu" product="air" /> does not support the <Cmd sid="column_filter_modal_title" product="air" /> feature.

:::

### Filter logs {#filtering-event-logs}

Filter logs to quickly find the information you need. Click the <IcAirLogfilter width="20" height="20" title="Filter" /> button at the top of the log list. When the <Cmd sid="logs_filters_action_btn" product="air" /> window appears, select the desired value for each item.

<Image src="/img/air/bsair-monitoring-eventlog-list-filter.png" />

:::info

* Click <Cmd sid="column_filter_modal_save_btn" product="air" /> or <Cmd sid="filters_action2" product="air" /> to save the settings so they remain after navigating to another menu. Click <Cmd sid="column_filter_modal_apply_btn" product="air" /> or <Cmd sid="filters_action3" product="air" /> to apply the changes only to the current list; they will revert when you navigate away.

* Click <Cmd sid="column_filter_modal_reset_btn" product="air" /> or <Cmd sid="filters_action1" product="air" /> to cancel the changes and return to the initial state.

:::

### Configure reports {#generatingReports}

Generate reports for events that occurred during a specific period by filtering log data.

1. Click the <IcAirReport width="20" height="20" title="Report" /> button at the top of the log list.

2. When the <Cmd sid="report_popup_title" product="air" /> window appears, enter a report name.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-monitoring-eventlog-report-setting.png" caption />

3. Select the items to include in the report.

4. Click <Cmd sid="report_form_text_add" product="air" /> to save the configured report.

5. A report is created with the name set in <Cmd sid="report_form_text_title" product="air" />.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-monitoring-eventlog-report-added.png" caption />

6. To download the generated report, click the <IcAirDown width="20" height="20" title="Download" /> button next to the report name.

7. When the <Cmd sid="report_download_popup_title" product="air" /> window appears, select the period to query events and click <Cmd sid="report_download_popup_button_title" product="air" />.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-monitoring-eventlog-report-download.png" caption />

A download link is sent to the administrator's email.

:::info

<Cmd sid="report_form_text_title" product="air" /> lets you search for, edit, download, or delete the reports created.

:::

### Mask personal data {#dataMasking}

Mask personal data in logs to protect sensitive information. When personal data masking is enabled, information such as user names, email addresses, and phone numbers is partially hidden.

To enable personal data masking, click the <IcAirMask width="20" height="20" title="Apply Masking" /> button at the top of the log list. In the pop-up menu, enable <Cmd sid="apply_masking_title" product="air" /> and click <Cmd sid="save_btn" product="air" />.

<Image src="/img/air/bsair-monitoring-eventlog-apply-masking.png" caption />

### Use the toolbar {#usingToolbar}

Use the toolbar at the top of the log list to manage logs more efficiently.

* To sort logs by date, select a date and time range, then click <Cmd sid="column_filter_modal_apply_btn" product="air" />. Click <Cmd sid="column_filter_modal_reset_btn" product="air" /> to reset the date.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-monitoring-eventlog-set-datetime.png" />

   :::note

   <Cmd sid="audit_logs_submenu" product="air" /> automatically filters logs when you select a start and end date.

   :::

* In the <Cmd sid="search_placeholder" product="air" /> input field, enter specific keywords to search logs. Enter one of the following: RF card ID, user ID, email, or name.

   :::note

   <Cmd sid="audit_logs_submenu" product="air" /> does not support the search function.

   :::

* Click <IcAirDown width="20" height="20" title="Export" /> to save logs as a CSV or PDF file.

* Click <IcAirRef width="20" height="20" title="Refresh" /> at the top of the list to refresh logs.

* To refresh at a specific interval, select 5 seconds or 1 minute in <Cmd sid="auto_refresh_btn" product="air" /> at the bottom left of the list.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-monitoring-eventlog-auto-refresh.png" />

   :::note

   <Cmd sid="audit_logs_submenu" product="air" /> does not support the refresh function.

   :::

## Use event logs on mobile {#usingEventLogsOnMobile}

<Image src="/img/air/bsair-mobile-monitoring-eventlog.png" className="none" />

1. Open the BioStar Air app on your mobile device and log in.

2. Tap <Cmd sid="monitoring_menu" product="air" /> at the bottom of the home screen.

3. Tap the <Cmd sid="event_logs_submenu" product="air" /> card.

4. Scroll through the event log list to view its contents.

:::info

* <Cmd sid="logs_filters_action_btn" product="air" />: Filter event logs.

* <IcSearch width="20" height="20" title="Search" />: Search by specific keywords. Enter one of the following: RF card ID, user ID, email, or name.

* <IcAirReportApp width="20" height="20" title="Report" />: Generate reports. For more information on report creation, refer to [the following](#generatingReports).

* <IcAirDown width="20" height="20" title="Download" />: Download reports as CSV or PDF files.

* <IcAirMask width="20" height="20" title="Apply Masking" />: Apply personal data masking.

:::
