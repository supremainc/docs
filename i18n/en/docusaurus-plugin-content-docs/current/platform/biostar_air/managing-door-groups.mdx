Door groups provide a way to organize access points systematically in BioStar Air. Grouping doors simplifies access level configuration and system management.

:::info

For more information on device group management, refer to [the following](manage-device-group).

:::

<CustomerNote />

## Add door group {#addingDoorGroups}

1. Click <Cmd sid="door_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="door_groups_submenu" product="air" /> in the left sidebar.

2. Click <Cmd sid="doors_group_add_btn" product="air" /> at the top right of the screen.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-door-group.png" />

3. When the <Cmd sid="doors_group_title_add_popup_overlay" product="air" /> window appears, enter the door group name.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-door-group-add.png" />

   :::note

   Click <Cmd sid="device_group_label_checkbox" product="air" /> and select the parent group to add the device group as a subgroup of a specific group.

   :::

4. In <Cmd sid="doors_group_label_add_device" product="air" />, select the doors to include in the group.

5. Click <Cmd sid="btn_ok" product="air" /> to save the door group.

The door group list shows the newly added group and the number of doors it contains. Click a door group to view the doors it contains.

<Image src="/img/air/bsair-door-group-added-group.png" />

:::info

* Doors added to a subgroup are not automatically included in the parent group.

* Add an empty parent group without devices or doors.

:::

## Rename door group {#renamingDoorGroups}

1. Click <Cmd sid="door_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="door_groups_submenu" product="air" /> in the left sidebar.

2. Check the checkbox for the door group you wish to rename in the list.

3. Click <IcAirEdit /> at the top of the door group list.

4. When the <Cmd sid="doors_group_title_edit_popup_overlay" product="air" /> window appears, enter the new group name.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-door-group-edit-name.png" />

5. Click <Cmd sid="btn_ok" product="air" /> to save the new group name.

## Delete door group {#deletingDoorGroups}

1. Click <Cmd sid="door_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="door_groups_submenu" product="air" /> in the left sidebar.

2. Check the checkbox for the door group you wish to delete in the list.

3. Click <IcAirRemove /> at the top of the door group list.

4. When a warning message appears, click <Cmd sid="btn_yes" product="air" />.

The selected door group is removed from the list. Its subgroups are also deleted. Deleting a group does not delete its doors.
