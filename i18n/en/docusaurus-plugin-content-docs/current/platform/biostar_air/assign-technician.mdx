Partners can assign technicians to customer sites and set permissions. Manage granular access levels for each technician to protect sensitive personal information.

**Key features of assigning technicians to sites**

* **Granular access settings**: Flexible options from device access to full administrator rights

* **User data protection**: Comply with privacy regulations by using the option to exclude user data access

* **Efficient management**: Manage technicians at each site according to partner-defined permission levels

:::info

* Before assigning technicians to a site, invite their accounts in the Partner Portal. For more information, see <DocLink docId='platform/biostar_air/technician-access' />.

* For more information on assigning technicians to a site in the BioStar Air administrator portal, see <DocLink docId='platform/biostar_air/managing-technician' />.

:::

## Assign technicians to a site

After inviting a technician in the Partner Portal, assign the technician to each individual site so they can access those sites. Follow the steps below to assign a technician to a site.

<Steps>
<Step>

### Go to the site list

1. Log in to the [Partner Portal](https://partner.biostarair.com).

2. Click <Cmd sid="menu.label.siteList" product="pn" /> in the left sidebar.

3. In the site list, click the site to which you want to assign a technician.

4. When the <Cmd sid="siteListPage.drawer.edit.title" product="pn" /> panel appears, select the <Cmd sid="site.admin_technicans.title" product="pn" /> tab, then the <Cmd sid="site.technicans.title" product="pn" /> tab.

   <Image src='/img/air/bsair-partner-admin-sitelist-edit-technician.png' />

5. Click <Cmd sid="common.button.invite" product="pn" />.

6. When the <Cmd sid="inviteTechniciansModal.title" product="pn" /> window appears, select the desired account from the <Cmd sid="inviteTechnicianModal.distributor_admins" product="pn" /> or <Cmd sid="inviteTechnicianModal.distributor_technicians" product="pn" /> list.

   <Image src='/img/air/bsair-partner-admin-sitelist-edit-technician-invite-list.png' width='60%' />

Selected accounts appear in the <Cmd sid="inviteTechnicianModal.selected" product="pn" /> list at the bottom.

<Image src='/img/air/bsair-partner-admin-sitelist-edit-technician-invite-list-selected.png' width='60%' />

:::info

* Enter an administrator's or technician's name in the name search field.

* To cancel the selected account, in the <Cmd sid='inviteTechnicianModal.selected' product='pn' /> list, click <IcClose title='Remove' /> to the right of the account.

* Accounts that are registered as site administrators or already assigned as technicians to the site will not appear in the list.

:::

</Step>
<Step>

### Set site access permissions

After selecting all technicians you want to invite, apply the site access permissions. Available permissions are as follows.

* <Cmd sid='inviteTechnicianModal.dropdown_opt1' product='pn' />: Can be added to the site but cannot log in.

* <Cmd sid='inviteTechnicianModal.dropdown_opt2' product='pn' />: Can only access the <Cmd sid='dashboard_menu' product='air' /> and <Cmd sid='device_menu' product='air' /> menus.

* <Cmd sid='inviteTechnicianModal.dropdown_opt3' product='pn' />: Can access all menus except the <Cmd sid='user_menu' product='air' /> menu, but access to certain settings is restricted.

* <Cmd sid='inviteTechnicianModal.dropdown_opt4' product='pn' />: Can access all menus including the <Cmd sid='user_menu' product='air' /> menu, but access to certain settings is restricted.

:::info

* <Cmd sid='inviteTechnicianModal.dropdown_opt3' product='pn' /> and <Cmd sid='inviteTechnicianModal.dropdown_opt4' product='pn' /> have view-only access to the following menu path.

  * <Cmd sid='settings_menu' product='air' /> → <Cmd sid='title_techincians_settings' product='air' />

  * <Cmd sid='settings_menu' product='air' /> → <Cmd sid='site_submenu' product='air' />

* Once a technician is assigned to a site and permissions are set in the Partner Portal, only an administrator account can change those permissions in the BioStar Air admin portal.

:::

#### Set permissions for individual technicians

Assign different permissions to individual technicians. To the right of each account, select the desired permission level.

<Image src='/img/air/bsair-partner-admin-sitelist-edit-technician-invite-list-selected-each.png' />

#### Apply the same permissions to all technicians

1. Choose the desired permission level from the <Cmd sid='inviteTechnicianModal.apply_site_permissions_to_all' product='pn' /> option, then click the toggle on the right to apply the same permissions to all selected accounts.

   <Image src='/img/air/bsair-partner-admin-sitelist-edit-technician-invite-list-selected-batch-01.png' />

2. When the confirmation message appears, click <Cmd sid='common.button.yes' product='pn' />.

3. The selected permission is applied to all accounts in the <Cmd sid='inviteTechnicianModal.selected' product='pn' /> list.

   <Image src='/img/air/bsair-partner-admin-sitelist-edit-technician-invite-list-selected-batch-02.png' />

</Step>
<Step>

### Technician invitation completed

1. After completing technician invitations and permission settings, click <Cmd sid='common.button.confirm' product='pn' /> in the <Cmd sid='inviteTechniciansModal.title' product='pn' /> window.

2. The <Cmd sid='inviteTechniciansModal.title' product='pn' /> window closes, and the invited technicians are listed under <Cmd sid='siteListPage.drawer.edit.profile.invitations.title' product='pn' /> in the <Cmd sid='siteListPage.drawer.edit.title' product='pn' /> panel.

   <Image src='/img/air/bsair-partner-admin-sitelist-edit-technician-invited-list.png' />

3. To send invitation emails and allow technicians to access the site, click <Cmd sid='common.button.apply' product='pn' /> at the bottom of the <Cmd sid='siteListPage.drawer.edit.title' product='pn' /> panel.

</Step>
</Steps>

## Check invitation status

Check the invitation status of technicians invited to the site. The invitation status is shown as <Cmd sid='adminLCM.pending_status' product='pn' /> until the invited technician accepts the invitation. Invited technicians have up to 7 days to accept the invitation. After 7 days, the invitation expires and the status changes to <Cmd sid='adminLCM.user_status_expired' product='pn' />.

1. In the site list, click the site where you invited the technician.

2. When the <Cmd sid="siteListPage.drawer.edit.title" product="pn" /> panel appears, select the <Cmd sid="site.admin_technicans.title" product="pn" /> tab, then the <Cmd sid="site.technicans.title" product="pn" /> tab.

3. <Cmd sid='site.technicans.title' product='pn' />: Check the invitation status.

When a technician completes signing up for the site, their status changes from <Cmd sid='adminLCM.pending_status' product='pn' /> to <Cmd sid='adminLCM.user_status_accepted' product='pn' />.

<Image src='/img/air/bsair-partner-admin-sitelist-edit-technician-status.png' />

## Invitation revoked

You can cancel the invitation if the invited technician's email address was entered incorrectly, or if the issue has been resolved and the invitation is no longer needed.

1. In the site list, click the site where you invited the technician.

2. When the <Cmd sid="siteListPage.drawer.edit.title" product="pn" /> panel appears, select the <Cmd sid="site.admin_technicans.title" product="pn" /> tab, then the <Cmd sid="site.technicans.title" product="pn" /> tab.

3. In <Cmd sid='site.technicans.title' product='pn' />, for the technician whose status is <Cmd sid='adminLCM.pending_status' product='pn' />, click <IcAirMore title='More' /> at the far right.

   <Image src='/img/air/bsair-partner-admin-sitelist-edit-technician-reinvitation.png' />

4. Click <Cmd sid='adminLCM.revoke_invitation' product='pn' /> in the popup menu.

5. If a popup message appears, review the message and click <Cmd sid='common.button.yes' product='pn' />.

6. When the success message for the canceled invitation appears, click <Cmd sid='common.button.close' product='pn' />.

When an invitation is canceled, the technician's status changes from <Cmd sid='adminLCM.pending_status' product='pn' /> to <Cmd sid='adminLCM.user_status_revoked' product='pn' />.

## Resend invitation link

Resend the invitation link if an invited technician didn't receive the invitation email or has lost it.

1. In the site list, click the site where you invited the technician.

2. When the <Cmd sid="siteListPage.drawer.edit.title" product="pn" /> panel appears, select the <Cmd sid="site.admin_technicans.title" product="pn" /> tab, then the <Cmd sid="site.technicans.title" product="pn" /> tab.

3. In <Cmd sid='site.technicans.title' product='pn' />, for the technician whose status is <Cmd sid='adminLCM.pending_status' product='pn' />, <Cmd sid='adminLCM.user_status_expired' product='pn' />, or <Cmd sid='adminLCM.user_status_revoked' product='pn' />, click <IcAirMore title='More' /> at the far right.

   <Image src='/img/air/bsair-partner-admin-sitelist-edit-technician-reinvitation.png' />

4. Click <Cmd sid='adminLCM.resend_invitation_link' product='pn' /> in the popup menu.

5. If a popup message appears, review the message and click <Cmd sid='common.button.yes' product='pn' />.

6. When the success message for resending the invitation link appears, click <Cmd sid='common.button.close' product='pn' />.

## Lock or unlock technician accounts

Lock or unlock accounts for technicians assigned to a site.

1. Click <Cmd sid="menu.label.siteList" product="pn" /> in the left sidebar.

2. In the site list, click the site where you want to manage technician access.

3. When the <Cmd sid="siteListPage.drawer.edit.title" product="pn" /> panel appears, select the <Cmd sid="site.admin_technicans.title" product="pn" /> tab, then the <Cmd sid="site.technicans.title" product="pn" /> tab.

   <Image src='/img/air/bsair-partner-admin-sitelist-added-technician.png' />

4. In <Cmd sid='site.technicans.title' product='pn' />, click the toggle button in the <Cmd sid='adminLCM.column_admin_account_status' product='pn' /> column to lock or unlock a technician account.

5. Click <Cmd sid='common.button.apply' product='pn' /> at the bottom to apply the changes.

:::info

Newly registered technician accounts have site access set to <Cmd sid='common.label.locked' product='pn' />. To unlock the <Cmd sid='common.label.locked' product='pn' /> status, use an account with administrator rights for that site in the BioStar Air portal.

:::

## Delete technician accounts

You can delete the account of a technician invited to the site. When a technician's account is deleted, they cannot sign in to the site and their access is revoked.

1. In the site list, click the site where you invited the technician.

2. When the <Cmd sid="siteListPage.drawer.edit.title" product="pn" /> panel appears, select the <Cmd sid="site.admin_technicans.title" product="pn" /> tab, then the <Cmd sid="site.technicans.title" product="pn" /> tab.

3. On <Cmd sid='site.technicans.title' product='pn' />, click <IcAirMore title='More' /> at the far right of the technician you want to delete.

   <Image src='/img/air/bsair-partner-admin-sitelist-edit-technician-reinvitation.png' />

4. Click <Cmd sid='common.button.delete' product='pn' /> in the popup menu.

5. If a pop-up message appears, review it and click <Cmd sid='common.button.confirm' product='pn' />.

6. When the success message confirming the technician's account deletion appears, click <Cmd sid='common.button.close' product='pn' />.

:::info

Deleting a technician account does not remove the account entirely; it only revokes access to that site. Re-invite a deleted technician to the site to restore access.

:::
