Monitor the status of doors connected to BioStar Air in real time. Check whether a door is locked or unlocked and the status of its relay sensor, and lock or unlock it remotely to control access.

Refer to the following video to watch the video guide on monitoring door status.

## Monitor in the web portal {#monitoringWebPortal}

Monitor door status in the BioStar Air web portal. Click <Cmd sid="monitoring_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="door_status_submenu" product="air" /> in the left sidebar. View the list of doors registered to BioStar Air.

<Image src="/img/air/bsair-monitoring-door-status.png" />

:::info

* Register doors through device registration. For more information, refer to [the following](register-device).

* For more information on door management, refer to [the following](managing-registered-doors).

:::

### Door status indicators {#doorStatusIndicators}

Check the following items for each door.

* <Cmd sid="door_status_column2" product="air" />: Indicates whether the door is locked or unlocked.

* <Cmd sid="door_status_column3" product="air" />: Indicates whether the door is physically open or closed.

### Unlock a door {#remotely-unlocking-a-door}

Unlock the door remotely when it is online and connected to BioStar Air.

1. In the <Cmd sid="door_status_column4" product="air" /> column, click <Cmd sid="door_open_btn" product="air" />.

2. The status in the <Cmd sid="door_status_column2" product="air" /> column changes to unlocked.

3. After the configured time elapses, the door returns to locked.

:::info

For information on setting the duration that the door remains open, refer to [the following](managing-registered-doors#editingDoors).

:::

### Set always-open mode {#alwaysOpenMode}

Keep a door unlocked indefinitely. Use this when temporary or visitor access is needed.

1. In the door list, click <IcAirMore title="Control" /> at the far right of the door to keep open.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-monitoring-door-status-more-func.png" />

2. Click <Cmd sid="event_log_unlock_mode" product="air" /> in the popup menu.

3. The status in the <Cmd sid="door_status_column4" product="air" /> column changes to <Cmd sid="door_control_unlocked_description" product="air" />.

<Image src="/img/air/bsair-monitoring-door-status-control-always-open.png" />

Click <IcAirMore title="Control" /> → <Cmd sid="release_icon_tooltip" product="air" /> to return to normal operation.

### Set lock mode {#lockMode}

Keep a door locked regardless of the current schedule. Use this when enhanced security is required.

1. In the door list, click <IcAirMore title="Control" /> at the far right of the door to keep locked.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-monitoring-door-status-more-func.png" />

2. Click <Cmd sid="event_log_lock_mode" product="air" /> in the pop-up menu.

3. The status in the <Cmd sid="door_status_column4" product="air" /> column changes to <Cmd sid="door_control_locked_description" product="air" />.

<Image src="/img/air/bsair-monitoring-door-status-control-lock-mode.png" />

Click <IcAirMore title="Control" /> → <Cmd sid="release_icon_tooltip" product="air" /> to return to normal operation.

## Monitor door status on mobile {#monitoringDoorStatusMobile}

Monitor door status using the BioStar Air app on a mobile device.

<Image src="/img/air/bsair-mobile-monitoring-door-status.png" className="none" />

1. Open the BioStar Air app on your mobile device and log in.

2. Tap <Cmd sid="monitoring_menu" product="air" /> at the bottom of the home screen.

3. Tap the <Cmd sid="door_status_submenu" product="air" /> card.

4. View the list of doors registered to BioStar Air.
