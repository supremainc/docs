This document is a reference for device and panel settings in BioStar Air. Administrators can check basic hardware information, perform actions such as firmware update, reboot, or reset. They can configure operation settings such as network, authentication, card format, time and attendance, Wiegand, sensitivity, and connected modules. Available settings vary depending on the device model and panel type.
