The **Sensitivity Settings** feature lets users adjust the Bluetooth detection sensitivity between their mobile device and the reader compatible with BioStar Air. This feature helps ensure smooth authentication and reduces unauthorized access attempts and authentication failures.

:::info

Because Bluetooth signal strength varies by mobile device model, users may experience different detection ranges. Environmental factors such as walls, interference, or the placement of BioStar Air-compatible reader can also affect Bluetooth detection.

:::

## Adjust the sensitivity setting {#how-to-adjust-the-sensitivity-setting}

Users can fine-tune Bluetooth authentication sensitivity based on the mobile device’s Bluetooth strength and personal preference.

<Image src='/img/air/bsair-sp-app-set-advanced-sensitivity.png' className='none' />

1. On the Suprema Pass app home screen, tap <IcHamberg title='Hamburger menu' /> in the upper right corner.

2. Tap <Cmd>More</Cmd>.

3. Tap <Cmd>Advanced Settings</Cmd>.

4. Tap <Cmd>Sensitivity Settings</Cmd>, then move the slider to adjust sensitivity.

:::info

* Move the slider toward <IcMinus /> to lower the sensitivity and block unintended authentication.

  > For example, when the mobile device is on a desk 

* If authentication fails, move the slider toward <IcAdd /> to increase the sensitivity. However, the mobile device must be very close to the reader compatible with BioStar Air.

:::

:::tip

**iOS user tips**

Bringing an iPhone with Apple Pay close to the reader compatible with BioStar Air triggers Apple Pay. This occurs because the device’s NFC area is detected along with the mobile card authentication.

**Solution**: Adjust the sensitivity setting toward <IcAdd /> so that the reader compatible with BioStar Air detects the mobile device’s Bluetooth signal earlier, eliminating the need for physical contact with the device. This allows smooth access while blocking Apple Pay from being triggered.

:::
