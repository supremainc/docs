BioStar Air connects multiple types of secondary devices to the CS-20 primary panel. Support for RS-485, OSDP, and Wiegand enables easy integration with existing security systems. This article explains how to configure, manage, and remove secondary devices.

## Configure secondary devices

After registering the CS-20, configure secondary devices using one of the following methods:

| Secondary devices | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Suprema RS-485 devices | Connect XPass D2 and OM-120 via existing RS-485 wiring and standard communication protocols. |
| OSDP device | Uses a security-enhanced communication protocol |
| Wiegand device | Existing Wiegand-compatible devices, such as card readers |

### Configure secondary devices

Connect devices that support RS-485 communication to the CS-20 to configure them as secondary devices. Secondary devices appear nested under the CS-20 in the panel list.

1. In the panel list, click <IcAirMore title='More' /> at the far right of the registered CS-20 panel.

   <Image src='/img/air/bsair-device-panel-list-cs-20-more.png' />

2. Click <Cmd sid='add_slave_devices' product='air' /> to open the <Cmd sid='add_slave_devices' product='air' /> window.

   <Image src='/img/air/bsair-device-panel-list-cs-20-add-slave-device.png' />

   :::note

   If no secondary devices are found, click <Cmd sid='find_devices' product='air' /> to re-scan devices connected to the CS-20.

   :::

3. When connected secondary devices appear in the list, click the checkbox next to each device to select the ones to add.

4. Once your selections are complete, click <Cmd sid='add_btn' product='air' />.

Connected secondary devices appear nested under the CS-20. View all devices connected to the CS-20 at a glance in the panel list.

<Image src='/img/air/bsair-device-panel-list-master-slave.png' />

### Configure OSDP devices

[OSDP (Open Supervised Device Protocol)](../../reference/glossary#osdp) is a security-enhanced communication protocol. In environments requiring high security, connect OSDP-compatible devices to the CS-20.

1. In the panel list, click <IcAirMore title='More' /> at the far right of the registered CS-20 panel.

   <Image src='/img/air/bsair-device-panel-list-cs-20-more.png' />

2. Click <Cmd sid='add_osdp_devices' product='air' /> from the popup menu.

3. When the <Cmd sid='add_osdp_devices' product='air' /> window appears, select the port to add the device to.

   <Image src='/img/air/bsair-device-panel-list-cs-20-add-osdp-device.png' />

4. After selecting a port, click <Cmd sid='add_btn' product='air' />.

5. After completing the required settings in the <Cmd sid='device_cs20_add_osdp_devices' product='air' /> window, click <Cmd sid='save_btn' product='air' />.

   <Image src='/img/air/bsair-device-panel-list-cs-20-add-osdp-device-popup.png' />

### Configure Wiegand devices

Wiegand is a communication protocol widely used in access control systems. Connect a Wiegand device to the CS-20 to configure it as a Wiegand secondary device.

1. In the panel list, click <IcAirMore title='More' /> at the far right of the registered CS-20 panel.

   <Image src='/img/air/bsair-device-panel-list-cs-20-more.png' />

2. In the popup menu, click <Cmd sid='add_wiegand_devices' product='air' />.

3. When the <Cmd sid='add_wiegand_devices' product='air' /> window appears, select a port to add a device.

   <Image src='/img/air/bsair-device-panel-list-cs-20-add-wiegand-device.png' />

4. After selecting a port, click <Cmd sid='add_btn' product='air' />.

## Edit OSDP/Wiegand device information

1. Click <Cmd sid="device_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="device_submenu" product="air" /> in the left sidebar.

2. Click the <Cmd sid='device_slaves_panels' product='air' /> tab.

3. Click the primary device's + icon in the list to expand the list of connected secondary devices.

4. Click the OSDP or Wiegand secondary device to edit its information. 

5. When the Edit device information window appears, edit the details or settings.

   <Image src='/img/air/bsair-device-panel-legacy-device-edit.png' className='none' />

   * OSDP device: Set device name, network settings, authentication settings (RF Card, RF Card + PIN), and authentication timeout.

   * Wiegand device: Edit the device name.

6. Click <Cmd sid="save_btn" product="air" /> at the bottom to save the changes.

## Delete secondary devices

1. Click <Cmd sid="device_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="device_submenu" product="air" /> in the left sidebar.

2. Click the <Cmd sid='device_slaves_panels' product='air' /> tab.

3. Click the primary device's + icon in the list to expand the list of connected secondary devices.

4. Click the checkbox for the secondary device to remove.

5. Click the <IcAirRemove title='Remove' /> button at the top of the list.

   <Image src='/img/air/bsair-device-panel-remove-secondary.png' />

6. When the pop-up appears, click <Cmd sid='device_btn_confirm' product='air' />.
