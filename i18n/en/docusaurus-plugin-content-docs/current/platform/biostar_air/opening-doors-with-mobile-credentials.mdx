Use mobile credentials instead of plastic RFID cards to open doors with the Suprema Pass app. Tag the mobile device via Bluetooth or NFC, press and hold the card, or scan a QR code to open doors.

:::info

The Suprema Pass app serves as the user interface for the BioStar Air system and primarily acts as a digital wallet for mobile credentials. Users issue and store mobile access cards in the app to enter secure areas with their mobile devices.

:::

## Requirements and preparation

Meet the following requirements to open doors with mobile credentials.

* The Airfob must be installed. Airfob is an access control reader compatible with Suprema Pass mobile credentials.

* Install the Suprema Pass app on the mobile device.

  * [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/kr/app/suprema-pass/id6745057639)

  * [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.supremainc.supremapass)

* Enable Bluetooth or NFC on the mobile device.

* Enabling two-step authentication disables <Cmd>Background Mode</Cmd>.

* The administrator must enable QR authentication and attendance management in the BioStar Air portal.

* Verify that the site supports the hands-free access method.

:::info

* Location services are required to authenticate with the mobile access card in Suprema Pass. See the related FAQ at <DocLink docId='platform/biostar_air/location-services-issues' />.

* Follow the provided guidance to troubleshoot the issue if the mobile card authenticates with the device but the door does not open. See the related FAQ at <DocLink docId='platform/biostar_air/troubleshooting-suprema-pass-app' />.

:::

## Set connection method {#setCoonection}

Select between two operating modes in the Suprema Pass app. On the Suprema Pass app home screen, tap <IcHamberg /> in the upper right corner, then select the desired mode in <Cmd>Connection Method</Cmd>.

* <Cmd>Only while App is on Screen</Cmd>: Open the door only when the app appears on the screen and the mobile card is visible.

* <Cmd>Background Mode</Cmd>: Open the door even if the phone is locked when the app is running in the background.

:::info

Regardless of the mode, tag the mobile device on the reader to authenticate.

:::

## Opening doors

Use the Suprema Pass app to open doors in several ways.

### Tag your mobile device on Airfob

Bring the mobile device close to Airfob and authenticate via Bluetooth or NFC to open the door.

1. Make sure the Suprema Pass app is running.

2. Hold the Bluetooth or NFC antenna area of the mobile device close to Airfob.

3. The door opens when the mobile credential authenticates.

:::info

* Choose between two communication modes. For more information, see <DocLink docId='platform/biostar_air/suprema-pass-app-advanced-features' />.

  * **Bluetooth mode**: The mobile device communicates wirelessly with Airfob.

  * **NFC mode**: Available only on Android devices; tag the mobile device on the reader to authenticate.

* The Bluetooth or NFC antenna location varies by model. Refer to the device specifications for the optimal touch point.

:::

### Press and hold mobile card

Press and hold the mobile card in the Suprema Pass app to open the door.

1. Display the home screen of the Suprema Pass app.

2. Near Airfob, press and hold the mobile card for 2–3 seconds.

3. The door opens when the mobile credential authenticates.

:::info

* Select <Cmd>Background Mode</Cmd> in <Cmd>Connection Method</Cmd> to use this feature. For more information, see <DocLink inner='#setCoonection' />.

* For security reasons, **the site administrator may disable this feature**.

* **Effective range** varies depending on mobile device Bluetooth strength.

* Nearby access control readers may trigger unintended authentication.

:::

### QR authentication

If the administrator enables the <Cmd sid='site_setting_qr_label' product='air' /> option in the BioStar Air portal, you can open the door by scanning the QR code in the Suprema Pass app with a QR reader. On the home screen of the Suprema Pass app, tap the <IcSpqr title='QR Scan' /> icon under the mobile card.

<Image src='/img/air/bsair-sp-app-contact-qrcode.png' width='50%' className='none' />

:::info

* For more information about the <Cmd sid='site_setting_qr_label' product='air' /> option, see <DocLink docId='platform/biostar_air/site-additional-settings' />.

* QR authentication is available only at doors equipped with a QR reader.

:::
