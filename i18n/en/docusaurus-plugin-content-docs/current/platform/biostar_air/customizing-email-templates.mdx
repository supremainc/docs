With BioStar Air's email template feature, customize the emails sent to site users to reflect your company's branding and message. Email templates include invitations, activation emails, and other notifications.

:::tip

Watch the following video to learn how to configure email templates.

<YouTube videoId="xmgpp6xMBuE" />

:::

## Add email templates {#addEmailTemplate}

1. Click <Cmd sid="settings_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="email_templates.email_template_menu" product="air" /> in the left sidebar.

2. Click <Cmd sid="email_templates.add_template_btn" product="air" /> at the top right of the screen.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-settings-email.png" />

3. When the <Cmd sid="email_templates.add_email_template" product="air" /> panel appears, set each item. Select the <Cmd sid="user_text" product="air" /> or <Cmd sid="admin_text" product="air" /> tab to configure user or administrator email templates.

:::info

Create separate email templates for each language. Select the language in <Cmd sid="user_form_input3" product="air" />. Supported languages are as follows:

> English, 한국어, 日本語, Nederlands, Español, Português

More languages will be supported.

:::

### <Cmd sid="user_text" product="air" className="normal" /> {#user}

Customize the email templates sent to users.

<Image src="/img/air/bsair-settings-email-add-user.png" />

* <Cmd sid="email_templates.email_type" product="air" />: Select the email template type to create. Available options are as follows:

   * <Cmd sid="email_templates.activate_user" product="air" />: Typically used as an invitation to download a mobile card.

   * <Cmd sid="email_templates.reactivate_user" product="air" />: Sends a notification to the user that the mobile card has been reactivated.

   * <Cmd sid="email_templates.reissue_user" product="air" />: Used when issuing a new mobile card.

   * <Cmd sid="email_templates.delete_user" product="air" />: Sends a notification to the user about card deletion.

   * <Cmd sid="email_templates.suspend_user" product="air" />: Notifies the user that the card has been suspended.

   * <Cmd sid="email_templates.upload_user_face" product="air" />: Used to request that the user upload a face photo.

* <Cmd sid="email_templates.card_type" product="air" />: Can be selected for email types that allow mobile card downloads, such as <Cmd sid="email_templates.activate_user" product="air" /> and <Cmd sid="email_templates.reissue_user" product="air" />.

   * <Cmd sid="user_card_type_mobile" product="air" />

   * <Cmd sid="user_card_type_web_link" product="air" />

   * <Cmd sid="user_card_type_web_qr" product="air" />

   :::note

   For more information on credential types, refer to [the following](user-card-types).

   :::

### <Cmd sid="admin_text" product="air" className="normal" /> {#admin}

Customize the email templates sent to administrators.

<Image src="/img/air/bsair-settings-email-add-admin.png" />

<Cmd sid="email_templates.email_type" product="air" />: Select the email template type to create. Available options are as follows:

* <Cmd sid="email_templates.alert_event_log_admin" product="air" />: Sends administrators notifications about events occurring on the site.

* <Cmd sid="email_templates.alert_system_admin" product="air" />: Sends system alerts to administrators.

* <Cmd sid="email_templates.alert_report_admin" product="air" />: Sends administrators a download link after generating a report.

### <Cmd sid="email_templates.email_styles" product="air" className="normal" /> {#emailStyles}

Customize the email logo and content. View changes in real time in <Cmd sid="email_templates.preview" product="air" />.

<Image src="/img/air/bsair-settings-email-add-style.png" />

* <Cmd sid="email_templates.logo_image" product="air" />: Upload the logo image to appear in the email.

   :::note

   * File types: JPG, JPEG, PNG

   * Minimum resolution: 200 x 60

   * File size: 1 MB or less

   :::

* <Cmd sid="email_templates.title_text" product="air" />: Enter the email subject.

* <Cmd sid="email_templates.body_text" product="air" />: Enter the message to display in the email body.

   :::note

   The email body must include HTML keys. Each key is replaced with the recipient's email address, the site name, and other values. Available keys may vary according to the value selected in <Cmd sid="email_templates.email_type" product="air" />.

   * **User** tab

      * `${email}`: Email address

      * `${siteName}`: Site name

   * **Administrator** tab

      * `${email}`: Email address

      * `${siteName}`: Site name

      * `${alertType}`: Alert type

      * `${alertDetails}`: Alert details

      * `${frequency}`: Alert frequency

      * `${location}`: Alert location

      * `${userInvolved}`: User involved in the alert

      * `${deviceInvolved}`: Device involved in the alert

      * `${dateTime}`: Date and time of the alert

   :::

* <Cmd sid="email_templates.bottom_text" product="air" />: Enter additional information to appear at the bottom of the email. Add the company address or contact information for compliance or to provide location details to users.

:::info

* Verify that the logo image is in the proper format and size so that it appears correctly in the email.

* If the current language is not supported, check future updates. More languages will be added.

* The address field is important for regulatory compliance and clarity, so enter it accurately.

* Click <Cmd sid="save_btn" product="air" /> at the bottom to save the email template.

* Click <Cmd sid="new_button_reset" product="air" /> at the bottom to restore the original content after making changes.

:::

## Edit email templates {#editEmailTemplate}

1. Click <Cmd sid="settings_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="email_templates.email_template_menu" product="air" /> in the left sidebar.

2. Click the email template you want to edit from the list. Alternatively, select the checkbox and click <IcAirEdit /> at the top.

3. When the <Cmd sid="email_templates.edit_email_template" product="air" /> panel appears, edit the necessary items.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-settings-email-edit.png" />

4. Click <Cmd sid="save_btn" product="air" /> at the bottom to save the changes.

## Delete email templates {#deleteEmailTemplate}

1. Click <Cmd sid="settings_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="email_templates.email_template_menu" product="air" /> in the left sidebar.

2. Select the checkbox of the email template you want to delete from the list. You can select more than one template.

3. Click <IcAirRemove /> at the top of the list.

4. When the message window appears, click <Cmd sid="btn_ok" product="air" />Yes.
