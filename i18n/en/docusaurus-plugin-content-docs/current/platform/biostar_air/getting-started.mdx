The BioStar Air portal provides a unified interface across the web and mobile apps, delivering a consistent user experience. Through the BioStar Air portal, administrators can manage access control settings, monitor site usage, and seamlessly configure users and devices.

<CustomerNote />

## Access the portal {#navigating-to-the-feature}

Open a web browser and go to https://admin.biostarair.com to access the BioStar Air portal. When the login page appears, select **Global Server** or **EU Server** based on your location. Obtain an administrator account and have another administrator grant you permissions to sign in.

<Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-login.png" />

## Review the dashboard {#dashboard-overview}

After logging in, the <Cmd sid="dashboard_menu" product="air" /> appears and shows key site information.

<Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-dashboard.png" />

* <Cmd sid="user_tagged_today" product="air" />: View the number of users who accessed the site today.

* <Cmd sid="users_card" product="air" />: View the total number of registered and active users. Click the card to open <Cmd sid="user_menu" product="air" />.

* <Cmd sid="devices_card" product="air" />: View the number of registered devices. Click the card to open <Cmd sid="device_menu" product="air" />.

* <Cmd sid="credit_status_card" product="air" />: View the remaining account credits. Click the card to open <Cmd sid="settings_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="credit_center_submenu" product="air" />.

* <Cmd sid="dashboard_usage_header" product="air" />: Sort usage by device group, frequency (daily, weekly, monthly), or custom date range.

* <Cmd sid="recent_actions_header" product="air" />: Shows an activity log of administrator actions such as sign-ins, device updates, and user creation.

## Site setup guide {#step-by-step-guide}

Follow these instructions to set up a new site.

<Steps>
<Step>

### Add administrators {#adding-administrators}

Invite administrators.

1. Click <Cmd sid="settings_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="admin_submenu" product="air" /> in the left sidebar.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-setting-admin.png" />

2. Click <Cmd sid="btn_invite" product="air" /> at the top right of the screen.

3. When the <Cmd sid="admin_invitation_title" product="air" /> window appears, enter the administrator’s email address and assign a role.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-setting-admin-invite.png" />

4. Click <Cmd sid="send_btn" product="air" />.

:::info

Administrators can manage the site, whereas general users can access it only through the **Suprema Pass** app.

:::

<CustomerNote />

</Step>
<Step>

### Register devices {#registering-devices}

Review registered devices or add new ones. Click <Cmd sid="device_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="device_submenu" product="air" /> in the left sidebar.

<Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-device-mng.png" />

* If the device is not registered, ask another administrator to register it on-site with the BioStar Air mobile app.

* In <Cmd sid="device_submenu" product="air" />, delete devices, update firmware, and manage device groups.

</Step>
<Step>

### Create and manage doors {#creating-and-managing-doors}

Add doors and assign devices.

1. Click <Cmd sid="door_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="door_submenu" product="air" /> in the left sidebar.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-door-mng.png" />

2. If no doors are listed, click <Cmd sid="doors_group_label_add_device" product="air" /> in the upper-right corner.

3. When the <Cmd sid="add_doors.title" product="air" /> panel appears, enter a name and description.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-door-mng-add.png" />

4. Assign registered devices to <Cmd sid="door_product_column2" product="air" /> and <Cmd sid="door_product_column3" product="air" />.

In <Cmd sid="door_menu" product="air" />, edit or delete doors, configure door schedules, and manage them by group.

</Step>
<Step>

### Categorize doors into groups {#categorizing-doors-into-groups}

Categorize doors into groups.

1. Click <Cmd sid="door_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="door_groups_submenu" product="air" /> in the left sidebar.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-door-group-mng.png" />

2. Click <Cmd sid="doors_group_add_btn" product="air" /> at the top right of the screen.

3. When the <Cmd sid="doors_group_title_add_popup_overlay" product="air" /> window appears, enter a group name such as **Indoor**, **Outdoor**, or **Trap Doors**, and select the desired doors.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-door-group-mng-add.png" />

4. After selecting all doors, click <Cmd sid="btn_ok" product="air" />.

</Step>
<Step>

### Configure access rights {#configuring-access-rights}

Follow these steps to manage access rights.

#### Set up holiday groups {#setting-up-holiday-groups}

1. In the left sidebar, click <Cmd sid="access_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="holiday_groups_submenu" product="air" />.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-holiday-group-mng.png" />

2. Click <Cmd sid="holiday_group_add_btn" product="air" /> at the top right of the screen.

3. When the <Cmd sid="holiday_group_add_title" product="air" /> panel appears, enter a name (for example, "Korea Holidays").

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-holiday-group-mng-add.png" />

4. In the <Cmd sid="add_holidays_title" product="air" /> section, set the holidays on which to restrict access, and click <Cmd sid="add_btn" product="air" />.

5. After adding all holidays, click <Cmd sid="save_btn" product="air" />.

#### Create schedules {#creating-schedules}

1. Click <Cmd sid="access_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="schedules_submenu" product="air" /> in the left sidebar.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-schedule.png" />

2. Click <Cmd sid="schedules_add_btn" product="air" /> at the top right of the screen.

3. When the <Cmd sid="schedule_add_title" product="air" /> panel appears, enter a name.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-schedule-add.png" />

4. Select the days to activate and link a holiday group.

5. When the schedule is complete, click <Cmd sid="save_btn" product="air" />.

#### Define access levels {#defining-access-levels}

1. Click <Cmd sid="access_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="access_levels_submenu" product="air" /> in the left sidebar.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-access-level-mng.png" />

2. Click <Cmd sid="add_access_level" product="air" /> at the top right of the screen.

3. When the <Cmd sid="access_level_title" product="air" /> panel appears, enter a name, and assign door groups, individual doors, and schedules.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-access-level-mng-add.png" />

4. When the access level is configured, click <Cmd sid="add_btn" product="air" />.

</Step>
<Step>

### Create user groups and add users {#creating-user-groups-and-adding-users}

Create a user group and add users to the new group.

1. Click <Cmd sid="user_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="user_groups_submenu" product="air" /> in the left sidebar.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-user-group-mng.png" />

2. Click <Cmd sid="add_user_group_btn" product="air" /> at the top right of the screen.

3. When the <Cmd sid="add_user_group_popup_title" product="air" /> window appears, enter the user group name.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-user-group-mng-add.png" />

4. Click <Cmd sid="save_btn" product="air" /> to save the user group.

5. Click <Cmd sid="user_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="user_submenu" product="air" /> in the left sidebar.

6. Click <Cmd sid="add_user_btn" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="add_user_btn" product="air" /> at the top right of the screen.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-user-group-mng-add-user.png" />

7. When the <Cmd sid="add_user_form_header" product="air" /> window appears, enter the user ID, name, email, and so on in the <Cmd sid="user_detail" product="air" /> section.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-user-group-mng-add-user-details.png" />

8. In the <Cmd sid="credential_detail" product="air" /> section, enable one or more credential types.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-user-group-mng-add-credential-details.png" />

9. In the <Cmd sid="access_detail" product="air" /> section, select an access level.

   <Image src="/img/air/bsair-gettingstarted-user-group-mng-add-aclevel-details.png" />

10. To finish configuring the user’s access, click <Cmd sid="add_user_card_activate_btn" product="air" /> in the lower-right corner.

:::info

In the <Cmd sid="user_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="user_groups_submenu" product="air" /> menu, bulk-manage users, reissue, suspend, or delete them, export data, and upload profile images in bulk.

:::

</Step>
</Steps>

:::tip

**Checklist**

To summarize, follow these steps.

1. Add administrators.

2. Register devices.

3. Create doors and assign devices.

4. Categorize doors into groups.

5. Configure access rights (holidays, schedules, access levels).

6. Create user groups and add users.

Following these steps sets up the BioStar Air site smoothly. Refer to the individual video tutorials in the user guide for more details.

:::
