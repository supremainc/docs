To add devices to BioStar Air, use the BioStar Air mobile app. Use the mobile app to register new devices, delete existing ones, and upgrade firmware over a wireless connection.

## Register Device

Use the BioStar Air mobile app to register devices. Register devices using Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), and manage them through the BioStar Air portal.

<Image src='/img/air/bsair-register-device-step.png' className='none' />

1. Open the BioStar Air app on your mobile device and log in.

2. Tap the <Cmd sid="devices_card" product="air" /> card.

3. Tap <IcAdd /> at the top right of the screen.

4. In the device search screen, tap <Cmd sid="button.register" product="2" /> for the device to register.

5. When the device registration window appears, tap <Cmd sid="device_btn_ok" product="air" />.

Return to the device list and confirm the added device.

:::info

* Devices cannot be registered on the web portal. Register the device via the BioStar Air mobile app.

* When registering a device, keep the device close to the mobile device and ensure that Bluetooth is enabled on the mobile device.

* Disable the <Cmd en="Create a door with this device">Create a door with this device</Cmd> option in the device registration window to avoid setting the registered device as a door.

:::

## Delete Devices {#removingDevice}

If a device appears offline in the device list on the BioStar Air portal, delete it using the mobile app.

<Image src='/img/air/bsair-remove-device-step.png' className='none' />

1. Open the BioStar Air app on your mobile device.

2. Tap the <Cmd sid="devices_card" product="air" /> card.

3. Touch and hold the device you want to delete. Select any additional devices to delete.

4. Tap the <IcAirRemove /> button at the bottom right.

5. In the pop-up, choose whether to delete the linked door.

6. Tap <Cmd sid='device_delete' product='air' />.

:::info

Devices other than X-Station 2 can be deleted from the BioStar Air portal. However, the device must be online. It is recommended deleting the device using the BioStar Air mobile app.

:::
