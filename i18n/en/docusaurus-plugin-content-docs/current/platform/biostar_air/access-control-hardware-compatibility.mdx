BioStar Air supports flexible device management for both distributed and centralized access control architectures. Devices can be registered, configured, monitored, updated, and operated from the cloud, allowing each site to use the best setup for its doors, wiring, and security requirements.

Sites can use edge devices that connect directly to the cloud, or control panels that manage downstream readers through RS-485 or Wiegand. Both architectures can be used within the same site, supporting new installations, biometric deployments, retrofits, and centralized panel-based configurations.

## Supported devices

BioStar Air is compatible with various access control devices and seamlessly integrates with devices in Suprema to enable cloud-based access control.

### Readers

* **XPass 2** (MDPB, GDPB, GKDPB)

* **X-Station 2** (DPB)

* **BioEntry W3**

* **BioStation 3**

* **XPass D2**

### Panels

* **CoreStation 20** (CS-20)

### Patch devices

* **Airfob Patch** (AP-H)

* **Airfob Tag** (AP-B)

### Other compatible devices

* **Output Module** (OM-120)

* **Secure Module** (SIO2)

* **Door Interface** (DI-24, coming soon)
