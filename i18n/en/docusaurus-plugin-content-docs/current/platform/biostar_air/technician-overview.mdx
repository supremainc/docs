You can grant access to customer sites using technician accounts. No permanent administrator account is required. Provide installers and partners with limited access to support system management and maintenance while maintaining security.

Technicians are managed centrally from the partner portal and can be assigned to individual sites. Access is managed through permission levels defined by the partner, ranging from device access to full administrator rights. An option to exclude access to user data is also available, allowing installation and support tasks while protecting sensitive personal information.

## Benefits of technician management

Technician accounts are designed for professionals who provide system installation, maintenance, and technical support across multiple customer sites. Partners can define each technician's role and permission level to achieve the following:

* **Enhanced Security**: Reduce the risk of unauthorized access by removing shared admin accounts

* **Compliance**: Meets privacy regulations; option to exclude access to user data

* **Efficient management**: Track and audit individual technicians' activities across multiple sites

* **Flexible permission control**: Granular permissions per technician and per site

## Limitations

* **Separate accounts required for the partner portal and the BioStar Air portal**: Each portal requires a separate sign-up process. Passwords are not synchronized between portals.

* **No expiration or time-based access control**: Technician access does not expire automatically. Site administrators must manually lock or remove access.

* **Fixed access permissions**: Only 4 predefined permission levels are available. Custom roles or granular permissions cannot be created.

* **Customer activation required when assigning partners**: Technicians assigned by partners must be unlocked by the site administrator before they can access the site. Lack of clear communication may delay support or installation.

* **No bulk assignment**: Technicians must be assigned individually per site.

* **Technician and administrator roles cannot share the same email**: An email address already registered as a technician in the partner portal cannot be invited as an administrator. When changing a role, you must delete the existing account and re-invite the user with the new role.
