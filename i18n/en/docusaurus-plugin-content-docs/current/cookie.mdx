Suprema Docs ([https://docs.supremainc.com](https://docs.supremainc.com)) uses cookies to provide its services and improve your browsing experience. This page explains the types and purposes of cookies used on the site.

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored in your browser when you visit a website. Sites use cookies to remember your language preference, analyze visit statistics, and provide other features.

## Types of cookies used

| Category | Purpose | Can be disabled |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Necessary cookies | Provide core site functionality such as language settings and security | No |
| Analytics cookies | Analyze visit statistics through Google Analytics (GA4) and improve content | Yes |

On your first visit, no cookie has been set yet, so the page appears in your browser's language automatically. When you select a language manually, the site saves that choice in a cookie and applies it on your next visit.

If you don't consent to analytics cookies, Google Analytics won't collect any activity from your visit.

## Changing your cookie settings

You can choose **Accept All**, **Necessary Only**, or **Reject All** from the cookie consent banner shown at the bottom of the site. Your choice is saved in your browser; if you clear your browser's stored data (local storage), the banner will appear again on your next visit.
