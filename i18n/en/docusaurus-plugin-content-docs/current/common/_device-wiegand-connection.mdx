Connect the product to a 12 V, 3.1 A power supply when connecting a Wiegand device.

<Image src='/img/device/installation-wiegand-in-connection-dm20.png' width='65%' className='none' />

:::info

You can use a 12 V, up to 1.5 A power supply when connecting a Wiegand device to the product. When connecting a device requiring 1.0 A to **WIEGAND 0**, only 500 mA can be used on **WIEGAND 1**.

:::

</Include>
