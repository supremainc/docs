## Registering in an Online State {#registering-online}

To activate the <Cmd>BioStar X License</Cmd> in an online state with an internet connection, enter your name and the provided activation key, then click the <Cmd sid='setting.server.license.button.activate' product='2' /> button.

<Image src='/img/biostarx-tutorial-license-online.png' />

## Registering in an Offline State {#registering-offline}

To activate the <Cmd>BioStar X License</Cmd> in an offline state, such as a closed network environment or when internet access is limited, follow the instructions below.

1. In the <Cmd>Activation</Cmd> section, click the <Cmd sid='setting.server.license.button.requestOfflineKey' product='2' /> button.

    <Image src='/img/biostarx-tutorial-license-offline.png' />

1. When the <Cmd sid='setting.server.license.title.activateLicenseOffline' product='2' /> window appears, click the <Cmd sid='setting.server.license.button.requestOfflineKey' product='2' /> button.

    <Image src='/img/biostarx-tutorial-license-offline-popup.png' />

1. When the dialog box appears, enter the <Cmd>Request by</Cmd> and the <Cmd>License Key</Cmd>.

    <Image src='/img/biostarx-tutorial-license-offline-popup-filed.png' />

1. Click the <Cmd sid='setting.server.license.button.download' product='2' /> button to download the license request file (*\*.req*).

1. Send the file to your local distributor.

When you receive the license file (*\*.lic*) from your local distributor, click the <Cmd sid='setting.server.license.button.activate' product='2' /> button and upload the license file.

:::info

If you do not have an activation key, enter only the <Cmd>Request by</Cmd>.

:::
