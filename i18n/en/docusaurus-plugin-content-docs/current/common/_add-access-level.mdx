1. Click <Cmd sid="access_menu" product="air" /> → <Cmd sid="access_levels_submenu" product="air" /> in the left sidebar.

2. Click <Cmd sid="add_access_level" product="air" /> at the top right of the screen.

   <Image src='/img/air/bsair-access-mng-ac-level.png' />

3. When the <Cmd sid='add_access_level' product='air' /> panel appears, enter <Cmd sid='schedule_name' product='air' /> and <Cmd sid='schedule_description' product='air' /> (optional).

   <Image src='/img/air/bsair-access-mng-ac-level-add.png' />

4. In the <Cmd sid="access_level_drawler_title_add_door_group" product="air" /> or <Cmd sid="access_level_drawler_title_individual_door" product="air" /> section, select door groups or individual doors.

5. In the <Cmd sid='schedules_title' product='air' /> section, select the schedule to apply to the access level. Select <Cmd sid="always_schedule" product="air" /> to allow unrestricted access.

6. Click <Cmd sid="save_btn" product="air" /> to add the access level.
