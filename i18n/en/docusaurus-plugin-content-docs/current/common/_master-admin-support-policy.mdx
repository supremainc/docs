### Master administrator support policy <Badge only>CE products vs Global (Non-CE) products</Badge> {/* #master-admin-support-policy */}

</Xclude>

The <Cmd sid="ui_128" product='dev' /> feature is activated only on <Cmd>CE products with a label that has the CE mark</Cmd>.

:::note

Use the sticker on the back label to identify **CE products** and **Global (Non-CE) products**. The feature works differently as follows.

* **CE products**: CE mark present

* **Global (Non-CE) products**: CE mark absent

:::
